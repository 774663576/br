第二十七篇: 一个真正的男子汉 One Real Man | 初中英语1600词汇故事

"It's good to see," said the king, "that we have one real man in the kingdom. Tell these chicken-hearted men why you alone among them stand on the right side of the hall."

In an ancient kingdom, all men were ruled by their wives. The king didn't believe it. In order to prove it was not true, the king had all the men of his kingdom brought before him. He warned that any man who did not tell the truth would be seriously punished.
