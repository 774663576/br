# Read and answer this question: Why is the new house special?
查看中文翻译
I had a letter from my sister yesterday. She lives in Nigeria. In her letter, she said that she would come to England next year. If she comes, she will get a surprise. We are now living in a beautiful new house in the country. Work on it had begun before my sister left. The house was completed five months ago. In my letter, 1 told her that she could stay with us. The house has many large rooms and there is a lovely garden. It is a very modem house, so it looks strange to some people. It must be the only modern house in the district.
