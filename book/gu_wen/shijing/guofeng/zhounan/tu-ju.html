兔罝 - Tu Ju

肃肃兔罝、椓之丁丁。

赳赳武夫、公侯干城。

Carefully adjusted are the rabbit nets;

Clang clang go the blows on the pegs.

That stalwart, martial man

Might be shield and wall to his prince.
