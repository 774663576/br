樛木 - Jiu Mu

南有樛木、葛藟累之。

乐只君子、福履绥之。

In the south are the trees with curved drooping branches,

With the doliches creepers clinging to them.

To be rejoiced in is our princely lady:

May she repose in her happiness and dignity!
