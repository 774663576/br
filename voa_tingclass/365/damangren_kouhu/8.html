Questions you can ask When you haven't seensomeone for a while.

一段时间未见某人时可以问的问题。

What have you been doing since I saw you last?

自从上次见到你以后，你都在忙什么了?

Have you been busy?

最近忙吗?

What did you do on the weekend?

你周末的时候在干嘛?

Did you do anything special this weekend?

你这个周末有没有做什么特别的事?

How was your holiday/weekend/trip/vacation?

你的假期/周末/旅游/假期过得怎么样?

How are you?

你好吗?

Are you well?

别来无恙?

How's it going?

近来如何?

How's business?

生意好吗?

What happened?

发生了什么事?

What have you been doing this week?

你这个星期都在做什么?

Where have you been?

你到哪儿去了?

Questions about someone’s future plans

关于某人未来计划的问题

What are you doing this weekend?

你这个周末要做什么?

Are you doing anything nice this weekend?

你这个周末要做什么开心的事?

Where are you going for your next holiday?

你下次休假要去哪里?

Any plans for…?

有打算要……吗?

Are you going out later?

你等一下要出去吗?

Do you always want to work in...?

你一直想去……工作吗?

What are you thinking of doing next?

你接下来想做什么?

Are you going to…?

你要去……吗?

Questions about something someone owns

关于某人拥有的某样东西的问题

Where did you get your…?

你的……是在哪里买的?

Was your …expensive?

你的……很贵吗?

What kind of computer do you use?

你用的是哪种计算机?

What kind of car do you drive?

你开的是哪种车?

What year is your car?

你的车是什么年份?

What color is your…?

你的……是什么颜色?

Where can I get a…like yours?

我在哪里可以买到跟你一样的……?

Questions about someone's experiences or career

关于某人的过去经验或生涯的问题

What did you major in?

你的专业是什么?

Where did you go to university?

你念哪里的大学?

Have you been to…?

你去过…吗?

Where did you work before?

你以前在哪里工作?

Where did you live before coming here?

你在来这里以前住在哪里?

Have you ever...?

你曾经…吗?

Questions about someone's views

关于某人的看法问题

What do you think of the current economical/political situation?

你对于目前的经济/政治局势有什么看法?

What do you think` of the long-term(economic/political/business) prospects?

你对于长期的(经济/政治/商业)展望有什么看法?

What do you think of…?

你对于……有什么看法?

What are your views on…?

你对于……有什么看法?

Have you seen this?

你看过这个吗?

Did you read that report on…in…?

你看了……的……报告吗?
