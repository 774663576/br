Me Neither

我也不

In class one day, Mr. Johnson pulled Johnny over tohis desk and said, "Johnny I have a feeling that youhave been cheating on our test."

一天在课堂上，约翰逊老师把约翰尼叫到了讲台前，说：“约翰尼，我感觉你在这次测验中作弊了。”

Johhny was astounded and asked Mr. Johnson toprove it.

约翰尼吃了一惊，他要老师拿出证据来。

"Well," said Mr. Johnson, "I was looking over your test and the question was, 'Who was ourfirst president?', and the little girl that sits next to you, Mary, put 'George Washington,' and sodid you."

“这样啊，”约翰逊老师说，“我看了你的试卷，其中一个问题是‘第一任总统是谁?’，你的同桌玛丽答‘乔治·华盛顿’，你也是这么写的。”

"So, everyone knows that he was the first president."

“那又怎么样，谁都知道他是第一任总统。”

"Well, just wait a minute," said Mr. Johnson. "The next question was, 'Who freed the slaves?'Mary put 'Abraham Lincoln' and so did you."

“等一下，”约翰逊老师接着说，“下一个问题是‘谁解放了奴隶?’玛丽答‘亚伯拉罕·林肯’，你也是这么写的。”

"Well, I read the history book and I remembered that," said Johnny.

“这能说明什么，我读了历史书，我记得这个，”约翰尼说。

"Wait, wait," said Mr. Johnson. "The next question was, 'Who was president during theLouisiana Purchase?' Mary put 'I don't know,' and you put, "Me neither.""

“等等，等等，”约翰逊老师又说道，“再下一个问题是‘路易斯安那购置期间的总统是谁?’，玛丽答‘我不知道’，而你的答案是‘我也不知道’。”
