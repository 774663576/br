英文原文

Success can be a lonely road,it can be a tough road,it can be a hard road.It's not for everyone.Those who follow others like sheep,we never know who they truly are.Only those who follow their own path can discover their true potential.

成功之路可能孤独，可能艰难，也可能坎坷。它并不适合每个人。那些像羊一样盲目跟随他人的人，我们永远不知道他们真正的面目。只有那些走自己道路的人才能发掘出自己真正的潜力。

Those who fly alone have the strongest wings.Those who walk alone have the strongest direction.The rest will always be in need of others for their survival,they will always need attention,need recognition to survive.

独自飞翔的人拥有最坚强的翅膀，独自行走的人拥有最坚定的方向。其余的人总是需要依赖他人才能生存，他们总是需要关注，需要认可才能活下去。

I am at peace alone,no attention, no recognition,and still I thrive.

我独自时感到宁静，无需关注，无需认可，我仍然茁壮成长。

I'm not saying those who have support are weak,I'm not saying you must go at it alone to gain strength.This is just for those who have fought battles alone,those who have felt like they don't fit in,those who have never had support in anything they do,all of those who feel no one believes in them。

我并不是说得到支持的人就软弱，我并不是说你必须独自奋斗才能获得力量。这只是对那些独自战斗过的人说的，对那些觉得自己格格不入的人说的，对那些做任何事情都从未得到过支持的人说的，对所有觉得无人相信自己的人说的。

You don't need them to believe in you,if you believe in you.You don't need their support if you've got your own back.The best part is when you truly live the life you want to live,when you speak your truth, when you embrace who you are,then you gain real respect and real love.

你不需要他们相信你，只要你相信你自己。如果你自己能够支持自己，那你就不需要他们的支持。最美好的是，当你真正过上你想要的生活时，当你说出你的真心话，当你拥抱真实的自己时，那时，你会获得真正的尊重和真正的爱。
