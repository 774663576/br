It's really very simple. The more you study, the easier your life will be. Keep studying. Do not ever give up. Respect the struggle of your journey, because it's the struggle that will build you to be stronger. But be persistent. Keep working on it; it's going to be a struggle, it's going to be hard. But that is an incredible thing, because you are going to figure it out. You will pull through. You have enough time, you have enough resources, you have enough commitment. You will find a way to smash your exams. Believe in yourself. You will figure things out. When tomorrow comes, you might not know what steps to take, but believe in yourself. It's your life. Take control.

其实这真的非常简单。你越努力学习，你的生活就会越轻松。坚持学习。永远不要放弃。尊重你求学路上的挣扎，因为正是这些挣扎会让你变得更强大。但一定要坚持不懈。继续努力，这将是一场斗争，会很艰难。但这是一件了不起的事情，因为你终将找到解决办法。你会挺过去的。你有足够的时间，你有足够的资源，你有足够的决心。你会找到击败考试的方法。相信自己。你会搞清楚的。当明天到来时，你可能不知道该采取什么步骤，但要相信自己。这是你的生活。要掌握主动权。
