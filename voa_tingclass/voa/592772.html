No.11: Be present in the here and now. 

第11条：活在当下。

We spend so much time dwelling on the past or planning for the future, that we forget to experience the present moment. But the past has gone, and the future hasn't come yet. The present moment is all we have. So, stop and look around you. See what is happening right now. And be mindful of where you are in this moment. By doing this, you will start to appreciate all that you have in your life, right now.

我们花太多时间沉湎于过去或计划未来，以至于忘记了体验当下。但过去已经过去，未来尚未到来。我们所拥有的只有当下。所以，停下来环顾四周。看看现在正在发生什么。留意你此刻所处的位置。这样做，你会开始感激你现在生活中所拥有的一切。

No.12: Appreciate everything in your life. 

第12条：感激你生活中的一切。

One of the most important life lessons is appreciation. The more we appreciate the things in our life, including people, experiences, lessons, and even our possessions, the more we'll be able to live in the present moment. So, be thankful for all that makes your life good. Because gratitude and appreciation is the key to happiness.

人生最重要的一课之一是感激。我们越感激生活中的事物，包括人、经历、教训，甚至我们的财产，我们就越能活在当下。所以，对你生活中所有美好的事物心存感激。因为感恩和感激是幸福的关键。
