I think that you can pick up from talking to me that I'm not on a different level, okay? These are pretty simple, basic tools. And so, nobody out there is listening to him and saying, "God, don't he's just... he's just using words and he's just so much smarter than me." So, I'm not gonna become worth $5 billion. That book is bullshit, okay? He's just naturally smarter than me. I think you can see that I'm no smarter than anybody else in this room. I just use certain little basic tools to be successful every day. 

我想你们从与我的交谈中可以感受到，我并没有高人一等，对吧？这些都是非常简单、基本的工具。所以，没有人在听完他的话后说：“天哪，他只是……他只是用了些词汇，他就比我聪明得多。”所以，我不会变得身价50亿美元。那本书是胡说八道，对吧？他只是天生就比我聪明。我想你们可以看出，我并不比这个房间里的任何人更聪明。我只是每天使用某些基本的小工具来取得成功。

Take the word "no" out of your vocabulary. Worry about your customer, no spare customers. Separate yourself from everybody else. Be the bullet, whatever you do. You want to be the bull. And I don't care if you're in the mail room or if you're the person behind that camera, I'm gonna be better than anybody else with that camera. And I'm gonna be the best mail carrier, and the person who comes up and is the most organized and does it the most professional way. You can be the bull at any position you're doing. And it's just telling yourself, "I'm gonna do this better than anybody else."

把“不”这个词从你的词汇表中删除。多关心你的客户，不要忽视任何客户。让自己与众不同。无论你做什么，都要做到最好。你要成为佼佼者。你可能是邮递员，可能是摄影师，我不在乎这些。重要的是，你要称为最优秀的那个人。告诉自己，我要成为最好的邮递员，成为那个最有条理、最专业的人。无论你从事什么职位，你都可以成为佼佼者。这只是告诉自己：“我要比任何人都做得更好。”
