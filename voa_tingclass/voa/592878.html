英文原文

You could be a rich man, but you cannot erase the work ethic part. There is no getting around it. You got to take the stands. Elevated don't go to the top man, not in the world of success. You gotta take the stands. Y'all gotta start getting gritty, man. 

你可能成为一个富有的人，但你不能忽视职业道德这一部分。这是无法避免的。你必须坚守立场。光有地位并不能使你登上顶峰，在成功的世界里更是如此。你必须坚守立场。你们必须开始变得坚韧，伙计们。

Somebody talked this to me a long time ago. He said, Steve, in order to get to the life of your dreams, you're going to have to learn how to get comfortable being uncomfortable. Because whatever your comfort zone is, if you stay in your comfort zone, that's where you are failed. If your comfort zone is this big, and you stay in your comfort zone, that's where you will fail. 

很久以前有人跟我说过这些话。他说，史蒂夫，要想过上你梦想中的生活，你必须学会如何在不自在中感到自在。因为不管你的舒适区是什么，如果你待在舒适区里，那你就会失败。如果你的舒适区只有这么大，而你一直待在舒适区里，那你就会失败。

In order to succeed in life, you have to step outside of your comfort zone. But I want to tell you something about yourself. All of you are equipped to live outside of your comfort zone. See, a lot of people are afraid to jump. A lot of people are afraid to take chances. A lot of people are afraid to put things up for risk. And you shouldn't be. Now, you can be afraid, but you should learn how to go ahead anyhow.

要想在生活中取得成功，你必须走出舒适区。但我想告诉你们一些关于你们自己的事。你们所有人都具备走出舒适区的能力。你看，很多人害怕跳跃。很多人害怕冒险。很多人害怕把事情置于风险之中。但你们不应该这样。当然，你们可以害怕，但你们应该学会无论如何都要继续前进。
