英文原文

Stop killing time,because eventually that turns around and time will kill you.You have the power to prevent time loss,you have the power to prevent wasted time,but you also have the power to kill time,and many of us are doing just that.

不要虚度光阴，因为最终时间将会反过来，吞噬你的生命。你有能力阻止时间的流失，你有能力防止时间的浪费，但你也同样有能力消磨时间，而我们中的很多人正是这样做的。

Before we make decisions, before we act,we need to think about how we're spending our time.We need to focus on what we want to accomplish,what makes the heart burn with passion,what wakes you up in the morning,what's your why.

在我们做出决定之前，在我们采取行动之前，我们需要思考我们是如何度过时间的。我们需要专注于我们想要完成的事情，什么让内心充满激情，什么让你在早晨醒来，你的目标是什么。

Time is the most valuable resource we have.I can save my money for later,I can't do that with my time.I can invest in more money,I can't do that with time.I can share my money,I still can't do that with time.

时间是我们最宝贵的资源。我可以把钱存起来以后用，但我不能把时间存起来。我可以投资赚更多的钱，但我不能用时间来做这件事。我可以分享我的钱，但我仍然不能用时间来分享。

Stop damaging your lifespan.Sooner or later,you're going to realize that the only thing you wish you had more of is time, but that's just what you threw away the most.

不要浪费你的生命。迟早，你会意识到，你唯一希望拥有更多的就是时间，但你却把它浪费得最多。
