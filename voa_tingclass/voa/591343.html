英文原文

Going wild,You love adventure, you love the outdoors.You love cooking hotdogs over an open campfire.We have just the vacation for you: camping, yes!

对于热爱冒险、热爱户外的你，喜欢在篝火上烤热狗的你，我们正好为你准备了这样一个假期：露营，没错！

For the brave soul who is not afraid of critters,bad weather, and bad food,this is another fun vacation choice.

对于那些勇敢无畏，不惧小虫、恶劣天气和糟糕食物的人来说，这又是另一个有趣的假期选择。

Camping requires a lot of very careful advance planning.You'll need to have gear for hot weather and cold,for rain and sunshine,and for grassy fields and rocky riverbanks.

露营需要非常仔细的提前规划。你需要准备适合炎热和寒冷天气的装备，应对雨天和晴天，以及草地和岩石河岸的装备。

If you're in the middle of nowhere,you'll need to carry your own food for all your meals,pots to cook it in,plates to serve it on, and utensils to eat it with.

如果你身处荒郊野外，你需要带上所有餐食的食物，烹饪用的锅，装食物的盘子，以及吃饭用的餐具。

That means a big and heavy backpack.You'll also need to carry around your own little hotel:a tent and the equipment to put it up.Don't forget a sleeping bag.I'm exhausted just thinking about it.

这意味着你需要一个又大又重的背包。你还需要带上你自己的“小旅馆”：一顶帐篷和搭建它所需的设备。别忘了带上睡袋。光是想想就让我感到疲惫不堪了。
