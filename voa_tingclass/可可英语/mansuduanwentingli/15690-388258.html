Rob was in Texas.
罗布在德克萨斯州。
He went to the post office.
他去了邮局。
He mailed a package to Jean.
他给珍寄了一份邮包。
He insured the package.
他给邮包上了保险。
Jean lived in New York City.
珍住在纽约城。
She lived in an apartment building.
她住在公寓大楼。
The apartment building has 20 floors.
公寓有20层。
It has a front desk.
公寓有前台。
The front desk is on the ground floor.
前台在公寓一层。
Security people work at the front desk.
安保人员在前台工作。
A week later, Rob's package was in New York City.
一周以后，罗布的包裹到达纽约城。
The mailman took it to the front desk.
邮递员将包裹送到了前台。
"I have a package for Apt. 3A. It's insured. Someone has to sign for it," he told Victor.
他对维克托说：“3A公寓的包裹。包裹上了保险。必须有人签收。”
Victor said, "I'll ring Apt. 3A to see if anyone is home."
维克托说：“我去按3A的门铃，看看有没有人。”
He rang the apartment. There was no answer. "No one's home," he told the mailman.
他按了公寓的门铃。没有人应答。他对邮递员说：“家里没人。”
"Would you ring again?" asked the mailman. "I don't like to redeliver packages."
邮递员问：“你能再去按一遍？我不想再来一趟。”
Victor rang the apartment again.
维克托又按了一遍。
Nobody answered.
没有人应答。

邮递员在珍的信箱上留了一张邮单。”
