He filled the pot with water.
他向水壶里盛满水。
He put the pot on the burner.
他将水壶放在火炉上。
He put a lid on the pot.
他在水壶上盖盖子。
He turned on the burner.
他打开了火炉。
He took a bag of coffee beans out of the cabinet.
他从柜子里拿出了一袋咖啡豆。
He opened the bag.
他打开了袋子。
He poured some beans into the grinder.
他将一些豆子放在研磨机上。
He ground the beans in the grinder for about 10 seconds.
咖啡豆在研磨机上研磨了大约10秒钟。
He took a No.6 paper filter out of the kitchen cabinet.
他从橱柜中拿出了一张6号滤纸。
He put the paper filter into a plastic cone.
他将滤纸放在塑料锥形体上。
A big glass pitcher was on the kitchen counter.
桌子上有一个大玻璃水罐。
He put the plastic cone on top of the big glass pitcher.
他将塑料锥形体放在了大玻璃水罐上。
He poured the ground coffee from the grinder into the paper filter.
他将研磨好的咖啡从研磨机里倒进了滤纸里。
He waited for the water to boil.
他等着水开。
He poured the boiling water onto the ground coffee.
他将开水倒进了研磨好的咖啡里。
The aroma of coffee filled the kitchen.
咖啡的香味弥漫在厨房。
Mmm! It smelled so good.
闻起来真香。
He couldn't wait to drink a cup of fresh, hot coffee.
他迫不及待的喝了这杯热咖啡。
