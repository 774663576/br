Neal's front yard was full of leaves.
尼尔的前院满是树叶。
He went outside and raked the leaves.
他耙平了叶子。
His cell phone rang.
他的电话响了。
He answered it.
他接起了电话。
Then he continued raking.
之后，他继续耙树叶。
He raked the leaves into three plastic bags.
他将树叶装进了三个塑料袋中。
The recycling truck came by an hour later.
一个小时之后，回收车来了。
The driver threw the bags into the truck.
司机将袋子扔进了卡车。
Neal wanted to call his wife.
尼尔想给他的妻子打电话。
He couldn't find his cell phone.
他找不到电话。
He searched his house.
他在屋子中寻找。
He searched his front yard.
他在前院中寻找。
Was his phone in one of the plastic bags?
手机是不是在塑料袋中？
He called the recycling center.
他呼叫了回收中心。
It was still open.
中心还开着门。
He drove there with his teenage daughter.
他和女儿一起驱车前往。
A 20-foot high pile of leaves was at the center.
回收中心堆集了20英尺高的树叶。
His daughter used her cell phone to call his phone.
女儿呼叫爸爸的电话。
At first he heard nothing.
起初，他什么也没听到。
She kept calling.
她继续打电话。
He kept digging through the pile.
他继续在树叶中寻找。
About 15 minutes later, he heard his phone ringing!
大约15分钟之后，他听到了自己的电话声！
He dug it out.
他将电话挖了出来。
"Don't tell your mom about this," Neal said.
尼尔说：“别告诉你妈妈这件事”。
