Kevin James is a lawyer.
凯文·詹姆斯是一名律师。
He has a popular name.
他的名字非常流行。
Many men have that name.
许多人都叫这个名字。
Kevin James is also the name of a major criminal.
凯文·詹姆斯还是一名罪犯的名字。
The Transportation Security Agency is looking for this major criminal.
运输安全局正在找寻这名罪犯。
TSA wants to put him in prison.
运输安全局希望将他绳之以法。
When Kevin James the lawyer flies to meetings, TSA pulls him aside.
就在律师凯文·詹姆斯要坐飞机参加会议时，运输安全局把他拉到了一边。
TSA questions him.
运输安全局询问了他。
TSA asks him if he is the criminal.
运输安全局问他是不是罪犯。
He usually misses his flight.
他经常错过航班。
"I always fly a day ahead of time," said Kevin.
凯文说：“我总是提前一天到达。
"That way, after I miss my original flight, I'll still be on time the next day."
要是我错过了第一架航班，我也会在第二天准时到达。”
Kevin told TSA, "Compare my picture to the picture of the criminal Kevin James. You'll see that I am not him."
凯文对运输安全局说：“与我的照片和罪犯凯文·詹姆斯的照片作比较，你就会知道我不是他。”
TSA said, "We don't have a picture of him."
运输安全局称：“我们没有他的照片。”
Kevin asked, "How can you capture him if you don't know what he looks like?"
凯文问道：“你不知道他长什么样子，那你们怎么抓他？”
TSA said, "Don't worry. We have our ways."
运输安全局说：“别担心。我们有自己的方法。”
