He had finally made it to New York City.
他终于来到了纽约城。
They call it "the city that never sleeps."
他们把这里称之为“无眠城市”。
He had always wanted to live there.
他一直希望能够在这里生活。
He had quit his job in Florida.
他辞去了佛罗里达州的工作。
He loved his new neighborhood.
他喜欢他的新邻居。
He loved his new apartment.
他喜欢新住处。
He had used half of his savings to pay for one year in advance.
他用一半积蓄交了一年房租。
But the apartment was so expensive.
但是公寓太贵了。
How could he afford it next year?
他明年该怎么办？
He had to get rich this year.
他今年一定要富起来。
"How are you going to get rich in just one year?" asked his sister.
他的妹妹问道：“你怎么在一年之内变得富有？”
That was a good question.
这是个好问题。
He didn't even have a job.
他还没有工作。
But an ordinary job wouldn't pay his rent next year.
但是普通工作交不上明年的房租。
An ordinary job wouldn't allow him to stay in his nice apartment.
普通工作不会让他住这么好的公寓。
He didn't want to move into a cheap apartment.
他不想搬进便宜公寓。
How can I get rich fast, he wondered.
他想我怎么能够迅速暴富。
He went across the street and bought a lottery ticket.
他过了马路，买了一张彩票。
