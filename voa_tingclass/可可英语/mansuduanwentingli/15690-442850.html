It was Jenny's fourth birthday.
那天是珍妮的四岁生日。
She was four years old.
珍妮四岁了。
Her mom baked a cake for her.
妈妈为她烘焙了一个蛋糕。
Her mom baked a chocolate cake for her.
为她做了一个巧克力蛋糕。
The cake had two layers.
蛋糕有两层。
Her mom put vanilla frosting on the bottom layer.
最底层是香草糖霜。
Her mom put vanilla frosting on the top layer.
最上层也是香草糖霜。
Her mom put vanilla frosting all around the cake.
蛋糕上下全是香草糖霜。
Now the chocolate cake was completely white.
巧克力蛋糕成了白色。
Her mom put four pink candles on top of the cake.
妈妈在蛋糕上插上四根粉色蜡烛。
Her dad lit the four pink candles.
爸爸点燃蜡烛。
The four pink candles were lit.
四根粉色蜡烛点燃了。
Jenny's mom and dad sat down next to Jenny.
爸爸妈妈坐在珍妮旁边。
They sang Happy Birthday to her.
并祝她生日快乐。
"Happy birthday to you," they sang.
他们唱起：“祝你生日快乐。”
Jenny sang with her mom and dad.
珍妮和父母一块唱起来。
"Happy birthday to me," she sang.
她唱到：“祝我生日快乐。”
Her mom said, "Now make a wish and blow out the candles."
妈妈说：“许个愿吹蜡烛。”
Jenny made a wish.
珍妮许下愿望。
Then she blew out all four pink candles.
之后吹灭了四根蜡烛。
