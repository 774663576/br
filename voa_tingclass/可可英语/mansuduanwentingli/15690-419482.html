She was 40.
她今年40岁。
She was tired.
她非常累。
She felt tired all the time.
她总是感觉累。
She went to the doctor.
她去看了医生。
The doctor asked, "What's the matter? What's your problem?"
医生问她：“怎么了？有什么不舒服？”
She said, "I'm tired. I feel tired all the time."
她回答道：“我感到非常累。我总是感觉累。”
The doctor examined her.
医生对她进行了检查。
The doctor asked her a lot of questions.
医生问了她许多问题。
The doctor asked for a blood sample.
医生要她进行血液检查。
He took some blood from her arm.
医生在她的胳臂上抽血检查。
The doctor asked for a urine sample.
医生让她做尿检。
He told her to pee in a cup.
医生让她在尿杯中取样。
A week later, the doctor called her.
一周后，医生将她叫道医院。
He said, "Your samples are okay.
他说：“你的血液检查没问题。
There is nothing wrong with you.
你没什么毛病。
You are tired because you don't exercise.
感觉累是因为你不锻炼。
You need to exercise.
你需要锻炼。
Take a walk every day.
每天进行徒步行走。
Ride a bike every day.
每天骑车。
Walk up stairs every day.
每天爬楼梯。
If you exercise, you won't feel tired."
如果你进行锻炼，你就不会感到累。”
She didn't believe her doctor.
她不相信医生。
She didn't exercise.
她不锻炼。
If I exercise, she thought, I will feel even more tired.
她认为如果我锻炼，我会感到更累。
