He called the cable TV company.
他给有线电视公司打电话。
"My screen goes black," he said. "Every five minutes my screen goes black."
他说：“电视屏幕变黑了，每五分钟我的电视就变黑一次。”
"Sir, does it go completely black?" the representative asked.
服务人员问道：“先生，请问是完全黑屏吗？”
"No," he said. "Big white letters say Please Wait."
他说：“没有，上面的白字显示说请等待。”
"Oh, that's good," she said. "If you don't see Please Wait, that's super bad."
她说：“啊，那太好了。如果你没有看见“请等待”字样的话，那就非常糟糕了。”
He was glad to hear that.
他很高兴听到。
"I'm glad to hear it's not super bad," he said. "So, does that mean someone can fix it soon?"
他说：“这并不是十分糟糕，这太好了。那么，是不是马上就会有人来修呢？”
"No," she said, "the soonest a technician can come to your home is next Tuesday.
她说：“不是，检修人员最快也要下周二。
If your screen was completely black, a technician could come to your home tomorrow."
如果你的屏幕黑屏的话，检修人员会在明天前往。”
He thought about that.
他想了想。
"You know, I'm watching my TV right now. It just went completely black. It does not say Please Wait. It's completely black."
说到：“你知道，我正在看电视。突然黑屏了。并没有提示请等待。完全黑了。”
She was silent for a moment.
她沉没了片刻。
"A technician can come out tomorrow, sir. Will you be home?" she asked.
她问道：“检修人员明天到达，先生，明天您在家吗？”
