Ellie spoke to the tour agent.
艾丽正在与旅行社进行交谈。
The price for the 3-night bus tour was good.
3晚的巴士旅行价格非常满意。
Her cousin Vicky called her.
她的表姐维姬给她打了电话。
Ellie told Vicky about the tour.
艾丽对维姬说了此次行程。
Vicky liked the price.
维姬认为价格不错。
She said she and her husband would come, too.
她称她和他的丈夫也会前往。
On Saturday, Ellie and her young son met Vicky and her husband at the tour agency.
周六，艾丽和她的小儿子在旅行社见到了维姬和她的丈夫。
Ellie brought snacks for the four of them.
艾丽为他们4个买了吃的。
Vicky didn't bring any snacks.
维姬并没有带任何吃的。
Ellie told Vicky to take some snacks.
艾丽告诉维姬吃点小吃。
Vicky didn't like the cookies.
维姬不喜欢吃饼干。
She didn't like the energy bars.
她不喜欢吃能量棒。
She liked the apples.
她喜欢吃苹果。
She put all 12 apples in her bag.
她在袋子里放了12个苹果。
She didn't leave even one apple for Ellie's young son.
她甚至没有个艾丽的小儿子留一个。
"These are not my favorite apples," Vicky told Ellie. "But they are better than those other snacks. Everyone needs to eat fresh fruit daily."
维姬对艾丽说：“这些不是我喜欢吃的苹果。但是它们比那些小吃要好得多。我们每天都需要吃新鲜水果。”
Ellie was polite to Vicky during the trip.
在旅途中，艾丽对维姬非常礼貌。
After the trip ended, she never spoke to Vicky again.
旅行结束后，她再没有与维姬说过话。
