It was Easter Sunday.
今天是复活节。
Mom gave a little basket to Michelle.
妈妈给米歇尔一个篮子。
Mom told her to look for Easter eggs.
妈妈让她去找复活节彩蛋。
The Easter eggs were in the back yard.
彩蛋在后院。
"What do Easter eggs look like?" Michelle asked.
米歇尔问道：“彩蛋长什么样子？”
Mom said, "They look just like regular eggs.
妈妈说：“它们和普通鸡蛋一样。
But they are colored.
但是它们带颜色。
They are red.
有红的。
They are blue.
蓝的。
They are yellow.
黄的。
They are many different colors."
还有一些其它颜色。”
"Why are they red? Do they come from red hens?" Michelle asked.
米歇尔问道：“它们为什么是红的？是红母鸡下的蛋吗？
"No," Mom said. "They are red because Daddy and I colored them red.
妈妈说：“不是，那是因为我和爸爸将它染成了红色。
They are blue because we colored them blue.
我们还把它染成了蓝色。
They are yellow because we colored them yellow."
还把它染成了黄色。”
Michelle asked, "Why did you color the eggs?"
米歇尔问道：“你为什么要给鸡蛋涂颜色？”
Mom said, "It's an Easter tradition. A tradition is something people do every year."
妈妈说：“这是复活节的传统。这是每年的习俗。”
Michelle went into the back yard.
米歇尔走进了后院。
She found six Easter eggs. "Next year you can help us color the eggs," Mom said.
妈妈说：“明年你可以帮我们涂彩蛋。”
"Oh, yes!" said Michelle.
米歇尔说：“太好了！”
