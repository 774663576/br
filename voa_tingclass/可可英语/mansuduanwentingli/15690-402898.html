His wife asked him to buy a cigarette.
他的妻子让他买包香烟。
"Excuse me?" he asked. "Since when do you smoke?"
他问道：“什么，你什么时候开始抽烟的？”
She said it was for the Chinese New Year.
她说这是为了中国的春节。
Her dead grandfather used to smoke.
她去世的爷爷曾经抽烟。
The cigarette was for grandpa.
烟是为爷爷准备的。
He went to the newsstand.
他前往了报刊亭。
He asked how much a pack of cigarettes was.
他询问了一包烟的价钱。
It was $11.
11美元。
He asked how much the cheapest pack was.
他询问了最便宜的烟多少钱。
It was $10.
10美元。
He asked if they sold single cigarettes.
他问道是否销售一支烟。
They didn't.
他们不卖。
He didn't want to spend $10 on a whole pack of cigarettes.
他不想花10美元买整包烟。
His wife had asked for only one cigarette.
他的妻子只要一支烟。
A woman nearby was smoking a cigarette.
站在旁边的女性正在抽烟。
"Excuse me," he said. "Can I buy a cigarette from you for a dollar?"
他问道：“对不起，我能花1美元来买你一支烟吗？”
He offered her a dollar bill.
他给了她1美元。
She gave him a cigarette.
她给了他一支烟。
She refused his money.
她没有要钱。
He felt lucky.
他感到非常幸运。
He had gotten a free cigarette.
他得到了一只免费烟。
And it was a Marlboro,the best!
烟还是最好的万宝路！
Grandpa would like that.
爷爷会喜欢的。
