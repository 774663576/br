Carl worked for a company for one year.
卡尔在一家公司工作有一年的时间了。
Then they laid him off.
他们解聘了他。
"We have to lay you off," said his boss. "It's not you. It's the economy. We'll hire you back when the economy gets better."
他的上司说：“我们不得不解聘你。这不是你的错，是经济的错。经济效益好些后我们会再聘用你的。”
Carl was angry.
卡尔非常生气。
It wasn't the economy. It's discrimination, he thought.
他认为这不是经济的问题，这是歧视。
They didn't lay off anyone else.
他们没有解聘其他人。
They laid off only him.
他们只解聘了他。
He looked for another job.
他正在找另一份工作。
He couldn't find another job.
他没有找到另一份工作。
A year later, he still had no job.
一年之后，他还没有工作。
It isn't my fault, he thought.
他想这不是他的错。
It was the company's fault.
这是公司的错。
They discriminated against him.
他们歧视他。
He bought a gun.
他买了枪。
He went back to his company.
他又回到了那家公司。
He shot his boss dead.
他开枪把上司打死了。
The police arrested him.
警方逮捕了他。
The murder was on the TV news.
杀人犯上了电视新闻。
The TV reporter talked about Carl.
新闻记者谈论了卡尔。
She felt so sorry for him.
她对他表示遗憾。
She talked about how much he had suffered.
她谈论了卡尔所遭受的痛苦。
She didn't say anything about his dead boss.
她并没有提及死去的上司。
