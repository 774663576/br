Jerry was looking at a map.
杰里正在看地图。
It was a map of the world.
这是张世界地图。
The map showed many countries.
地图上显示了许多国家。
It showed all the countries in the world.
显示了世界上所有的国家。
All the countries were different colors.
各个国家的颜色不同。
There were red countries, green countries, and blue countries.
有红色的，绿色的，蓝色的。
Jerry pointed at one country.
杰里指着某个国家。
"Daddy, why is this country red?
爸爸，为什么这个国家是红色的？
Is it on fire?
这里是不是着火了？
Did someone paint the ground red?
人们把地面涂成了红色？
Does it have a lot of tomatoes?"
那里是不是有许多西红柿？
Jerry pointed at another country.
杰里又指着另一个国家。
It was a green country.
国家由绿色代表。
"Daddy, why is this country green?
爸爸，为什么这个国家是绿色的？
Does it have a lot of grass?
那里是不是有许多草地？
Did someone paint the ground green?"
那里的地面是不是被涂成了绿色？
Jerry pointed at another country.
杰里又指着另一个国家。
It was a blue country.
蓝色的国家。
"Daddy, why is this country blue?
爸爸，为什么它是蓝色的？
Is it full of blue water?
那里是不是到处是蓝色的大海？
Does it have a lot of bluebirds?
是不是有许多蓝鸟？
Did someone paint the ground blue?"
有人把地面涂成了蓝色？
His daddy said, "No, the colors make the map pretty. They don't mean anything."
他的爸爸说：“不是，颜色让地图显眼多彩。它们不代表任何意思。”
