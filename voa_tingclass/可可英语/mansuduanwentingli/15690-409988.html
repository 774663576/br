It was the Chinese New Year.
这天是中国的新年。
The metal pan was light, shiny, and round.
金属盆又亮又圆。
It was about 12 inches across.
它有12英寸长。
It was perfect for burning paper.
正好适合烧纸钱。
She put the pan on the hardwood floor.
她把金属盆放在了木地板上。
She opened a package of paper money.
她打开了装有纸钱的袋子。
It wasn't real money.
这不是真钱。
"We burn the money for our ancestors," she said.
她说：“我们为先人烧纸钱。
"Then maybe money will come to us this year."
今年我们或许会收到钱。”
She took out some paper bills.
她拿出了纸钱。
She lit them.
点燃了纸钱。
She dropped them into the pan.
她将纸钱扔进了盆里。
She lit more bills and dropped them into the pan.
她又点燃了一些钱，扔进了盆中。
The living room got smoky.
客厅充满了浓烟。
Finally she stopped lighting the money.
她不再燃烧纸钱。
It finished burning in the pan.
纸钱在火盆中燃烧。
The pan of ashes cooled.
盆中的灰冷却了。
She picked it up.
她把纸钱捡了起来。
"Oh," she said.
她说“哦”。
He looked at the floor.
她看了看地板。
A dark ring was burned into the wood.
木板被烧成了一个圆形。
"Well, I hope your ancestors send us money this year," he said. "We'll need it to pay for the floor."
他说：“我希望先人能够在今年为我们寄钱，我们要用它来支付木板钱。”
