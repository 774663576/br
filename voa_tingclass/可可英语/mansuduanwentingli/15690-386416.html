Everything's a hassle, he thought.
他总觉得所有的事情都非常麻烦。
The smallest thing is a hassle.
小事情也非常麻烦。
Taking the cap off the toothpaste tube is a hassle.
打开牙膏帽是件烦人的事。
Squeezing the toothpaste tube is a hassle.
挤牙膏很麻烦。
Putting the toothpaste on the toothbrush is a hassle.
将牙膏放在牙刷上是一件琐事。
Brushing his teeth is a hassle.
刷牙很麻烦。
Life is just one hassle after another.
生活就是一个麻烦接着另一个麻烦。
"You always exaggerate," his wife said. "Not everything is a hassle."
他的妻子说：“你总是把事情想得那么夸张，并不是所有的事情都这么麻烦。”
He thought about that.
他想了想。
Could his wife be right?
他的妻子说得对吗？
Are some things not a hassle?
有些事情是不是没这么繁琐？
How about eating food?
比如说吃饭？
That's not a hassle.
这件事不麻烦。
How about taking a hot shower?
好比如洗热水澡？
That's not a hassle.
这不麻烦。
How about reading?
阅读？
How about watching a good TV program?
看一个好的电视节目？
How about sleeping?
睡觉？
He couldn't think of anything else.
他想不到其他不繁琐的事情了。
Eating, showering, reading, watching TV, and sleeping aren't a hassle.
吃饭、洗澡、阅读、看电视、睡觉不繁琐。
"Okay, I won't exaggerate," he said to his wife. "I won't say that everything is a hassle.
他对妻子说：“好的，我不会小题大做了，我不会说所有事情都这么麻烦了。
I'll say that everything except five things is a hassle!"
我会说除了五件事情以外，其他的都麻烦！”
