Brian didn't have cable TV.
布莱恩没有有线电视。
He didn't have satellite TV.
也没有卫星电视。
He had an antenna for his TV.
他有一根电视天线。
He had an indoor antenna.
是一根室内天线。
But it was an old antenna.
但已经很老旧了。
The reception was bad.
信号总是不好。
He needed to buy a new antenna.
他想再买一根天线。
He went to Best Buy.
他前往百思买购买天线。
The antennas were in boxes.
天线装在盒子里。
He looked at the boxes.
他看着这些盒子。
Which antenna was the best?
哪根天线最好？
He asked a clerk at Best Buy.
他询问百思买店员。
"Which antenna is the best?" he asked the clerk.
他问道：“哪根天线最好？”
The clerk said, "This is the best antenna."
店员回答：“这根最好。”
He showed Brian the box.
他将盒子展示给布莱恩。
Brian asked the price.
布莱恩询问了价钱。
"What is the price?" he asked.
他问道；“多少钱？”
The clerk said the price was $50.
店员回答50美元。
Brian took the antenna home.
布莱恩买回了家。
He plugged one end of the antenna into his TV.
他将天线一头插进电视机。
He plugged the other end of the antenna into the wall socket.
将另一头插入壁式插座。
He turned on his TV.
他打开电视。
The reception was bad.
信号还是不好。
He returned the antenna.
他将天线退了。
