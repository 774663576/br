She went to the casino.
她去了赌场。
A casino is a gambling house.
赌场是赌博的场所。
People gamble in a casino.
人们在赌场赌博。
They take a chance with their money.
他们用钱试试手气。
They hope they will win.
希望能够大赚一笔。
Sometimes they do.
有时真的如此。
But usually they lose.
但通常他们会输得很惨。
It was her birthday.
今天是她的生日。
She took $100 to the casino.
她带了100美金前往赌场。
She went to the change booth.
她走向零钱处。
She got change for her money.
她换了钱。
She got 400 quarters for her $100.
她用100美金换了400个二十五美分硬币。
The quarters were in paper rolls.
硬币用纸包着。
Forty quarters were in each roll.
每个纸卷有40个硬币。
She put the rolls into a plastic bucket.
她将纸卷放进了塑料袋中。
She carried the bucket over to a slot machine.
她拿着塑料袋来到了自动贩卖机。
She loved slot machines.
她喜欢自动贩卖机。
She opened a roll of quarters.
她打开了一卷纸。
She put a quarter in the machine.
将一枚硬币放进了贩卖机中。
She pulled down on the handle.
拉下推杆。
She looked at the screen.
她看着大屏幕。
Give me three cherries, she thought.
她想着，赶快出现3个樱桃。
Or give me three bananas.
或三个香蕉。
Or give me three apples.
或三个苹果。
Three of anything is a winner.
三种同类都算赢。
Two cherries, or one cherry, is a loser.
两个樱桃或一个樱桃都算输。
She played for several hours.
她玩了几个小时。
Sometimes she won.
有时她会赢。
She felt good when she won.
赢的时候感觉良好。
But usually she lost.
但她总是输。
Finally, she lost all her quarters.
她输掉了所有的钱。
She went home.
她回到了家。
She spent $100, but she had fun.
她花了100美金，但却很开心。
I will play again on my next birthday, she thought.
她说明年生日她还要来玩。
