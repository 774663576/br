"I don't like John," she told her husband.
她对丈夫说：“我不喜欢约翰。”
"Why don't you like John?" he asked his wife.
他问妻子：“为什么不喜欢？”
She said that John said bad things about Americans.
她说约翰说了有损美国人的话。
He said Americans are lazy.
他说美国人很懒惰。
He said Americans are greedy.
他说美国人很贪婪。
He said Americans are selfish.
还说美国人自私。
He said Americans are unfriendly.
美国人不友好。
John said the people in his country aren't lazy.
约翰说他国家的人不懒惰。
The people in his country aren't greedy.
不贪婪。
The people in his country aren't selfish.
不自私。
The people in his country aren't unfriendly.
也很友好。
"But John is new here," said her husband.
丈夫说：“约翰刚来到这里。
"He doesn't know anyone yet. He doesn't have any friends.
他还谁都不认识。他没有朋友。
When he has new friends, he will change his mind.
等他有了新朋友，他将改变想法。
He will say different things.
他将不会这么说。
He will say good things about Americans."
他会说美国人好。”
He told his wife to be patient.
他告诉妻子要耐心。
She didn't want to be patient.
但她耐心不了。
"A new person shouldn't say bad things," she said. "He should go back to his country."
她说：“新人不应该说坏话。他应该回到自己国家。”
