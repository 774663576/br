Kenny was crying.
肯尼在哭泣。
"Why are you crying?" Mom asked Kenny.
妈妈问肯尼：“为什么哭泣？”
"The cat scratched me," said Kenny.
肯尼说：“猫抓了我。”
The cat had scratched Kenny on his hand.
猫划了肯尼的手。
Kenny showed his hand to his mom.
肯尼让妈妈看手。
The scratch didn't look too bad.
抓痕不是很严重。
It wasn't a deep scratch.
抓痕并不深。
Mom went to the kitchen.
妈妈来到厨房。
Kenny went with her.
肯尼和她一起去。
She turned on the water.
她打开水龙头。
She put his hand under the water.
将手放在水龙头下。
She washed the scratch with soap.
并用肥皂清洗抓痕。
She rinsed off the soap.
之后将肥皂洗净。
She took a Band-Aid out of the kitchen cabinet.
从厨房柜橱中拿了一张创可贴。
She put the Band-Aid on the scratch.
将创可贴贴在抓痕处。
She said he would be okay.
她说这样就没问题了。
"Why did the cat scratch you?" she asked.
妈妈问道：“猫为什么抓你？”
Kenny said he didn't know why the cat scratched him.
肯尼不知道原因。
Just then, Kenny's little sister walked into the kitchen.
不一会，肯尼的妹妹走进厨房。
She knew why the cat scratched Kenny.
她知道猫抓肯尼的原因。
"I know why Tiger scratched him, Mommy," she said.
她说：“妈妈我知道原因。
"He was pulling Tiger's tail!"
她揪了猫的尾巴。”
