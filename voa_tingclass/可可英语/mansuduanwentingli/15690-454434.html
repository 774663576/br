"Daddy, let's order a pizza," Billy said.
比利说：“爸爸，点个披萨吧。”
"That sounds like a good idea," said his dad.
爸爸说：“听起来不错。
They looked at a menu they had at home.
他们看了看菜单。
"What kind do you want?" his dad asked.
爸爸问道：“你想吃哪种？”
Billy wanted a large pizza.
比利想要个大披萨。
He wanted a pizza with four toppings.
他想点一个有四个配料的大披萨。
He wanted cheese, pepperoni, ham, and pineapple.
他想要奶酪、意大利辣香肠、火腿和菠萝。
His dad said that sounded good.
爸爸说听起来不错。
He called the pizza place.
他给披萨店打电话。
"They don't have any pineapple," dad told Billy. "What do you want instead?"
爸爸告诉比利：“他们没有菠萝。你想要点其它的吗？”
Billy wanted sausage instead.
比利说他想要香肠。
His dad ordered sausage instead of pineapple.
爸爸没点菠萝而点了香肠。
About 30 minutes later, there was a knock on the door.
约30分钟后有人来敲门。
It was the pizza man.
是披萨派送员。
"Here's your pizza," he told Billy's dad. "That'll be $16."
他对比利的爸爸说：“这是您的披萨。一共16美元。”
Dad paid the pizza man.
爸爸给了他钱。
He also gave him a tip.
还给了他小费。
Dad took the pizza to the living room.
爸爸将披萨拿到了客厅。
A baseball game was on TV.
电视正在直播篮球赛。
Billy and his dad started to eat the pizza in the living room.
比利和爸爸一块在客厅吃披萨。
"I hope the Yankees lose," Billy told his dad.
比利对爸爸说：“我希望扬基输球。”
"I hope the Yankees lose, too," his dad said.
爸爸说：“我也是这么想的。”
