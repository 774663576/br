He was 58.
他58岁了。
He lost his job.
他失业了。
A friend offered him a job in Miami.
有位朋友为他提供了一个在迈阿密工作的机会。
He found a nice apartment in Miami.
他在迈阿密找到了不错的公寓。
He loved his new job.
他喜欢自己的新工作。
He loved Miami.
他爱迈阿密。
Life was good.
生活很美好。
Then his friend's company went bankrupt.
之后，他朋友的公司倒闭了。
Life wasn't so good.
生活并不容易。
He looked for work.
他开始找工作。
But everyone was looking for work.
所有人都在找工作。
There were plenty of workers.
求职的人很多。
But there wasn't plenty of work.
但是工作机会并不多。
He was almost broke.
他快要身无分文了。
He started coughing up blood.
他开始咳血。
He went to a doctor.
他去看了医生。
"You have lung cancer," the doctor said. "You need treatment."
医生说：“你患有肺癌，你需要治疗。”
He told the doctor he was almost broke.
他对医生说他快要身无分文了。
He had no money for treatment, rent, or even food.
他没钱治疗，没钱租房，甚至没钱吃饭。
The doctor said the government would pay for his treatment.
医生说政府会为他的治疗买单。
The government would find him an apartment.
政府会为他找到住处。
The government would give him food stamps.
政府会给予他粮票。
Why have I worked so hard all my life, he wondered.
他想，我为什么还要这么努力工作。
I should have gone broke 30 years ago.
我应该在30年前就破产。
