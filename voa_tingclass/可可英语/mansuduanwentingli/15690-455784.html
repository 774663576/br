Why do people do stupid things?
人为什么要做蠢事？
Some people try to walk across freeways.
许多试图穿越高速路。
They get run over.
但遭到汽车碾压。
Some people stand on the edge of cliffs.
许多人站在悬崖边上。
They slip and fall to their death.
不幸坠落悬崖。
Some people think a gun is not loaded.
许多人误以为枪没有子弹。
They point the gun at a friend.
他们将枪指向朋友。
They pull the trigger.
并扣动了扳机。
They kill their friend.
他们杀死了朋友。
Some people want to watch a storm up close.
许多人想近距离观察暴风雨。
They go to the ocean.
他们靠近了海洋。
They feel the wind.
他们感受到了海风。
They see the big waves.
和声势浩荡的巨浪。
They are excited.
他们非常兴奋。
Then a big wave takes them out to sea.
一个大浪将他们卷入了大海。
They drown.
他们淹死了。
Many people drop out of school.
许多人辍学。
They say that school is boring.
他们认为学校很无聊。
They want to have fun.
他们希望过有趣的生活。
School is not fun.
学校没意思。
They hate homework.
他们讨厌做作业。
They get a job at McDonald's.
他们在麦当劳得到了一份工作。
All day long they ask the customers, "Do you want fries with that?"
每天向顾客询问，想搭配薯条吗？
Is that fun?
这有趣吗？
No, but it is stupid.
当然不，这样做很傻。
Some people cheat on their wives or husbands.
许多人被判妻子或丈夫。
They get caught.
他们被揭穿了。
Their marriage ends.
婚姻破灭。
They are alone.
他们单身了。
Many people smoke cigarettes.
许多人抽烟。
They get cancer.
最终患上了癌症。
Many people drink and drive.
许多人醉酒驾车。
Drinking and driving is against the law.
醉酒驾车触犯法律。
It's dangerous.
且非常危险。
It's stupid.
这样做很蠢。
But people do it every day.
但每天都在上演。
