The teacher walked into the classroom.
老师走入教室。
He said, "Good evening, everybody."
他说：“同学们晚上好。”
The students said, "Good evening, teacher."
学生说：“老师晚上好。”
He walked over to his desk.
他走向讲台。
He put his briefcase on his desk.
将公文包放在讲台。
He opened his briefcase.
打开公文包。
He took his notebook out of his briefcase.
将笔记本拿出。
He took his books out of his briefcase.
将书本拿出。
Suddenly, he felt dizzy.
突然，他感到晕眩。
The room was spinning.
教室在旋转。
He sat down.
他坐下来。
What was wrong with him?
他怎么了？
He put his hands on the desk.
他将手放在讲台上。
The desk was spinning.
讲台在旋转。
He closed his eyes.
他合上眼睛。
He opened his eyes.
他睁开眼睛。
He didn't know what to do.
他不知道怎么办。
What was wrong?
怎么了？
He was sitting in his chair.
他坐在椅子上。
But he felt like he was floating in air.
但他感到自己在空中飘荡。
How could he be floating in air?
他怎么在空中游荡？
He sat in his chair for another minute.
他又在椅子上坐了一分钟。
His students were talking to one another.
学生们窃窃私语。
They did not know how he felt.
他们不知道老师怎么了。
But he didn't tell them how he felt.
他也没有说。
He didn't say anything.
他什么都没说。
