He walked into the drugstore.
他走进了药店。
Where are the eyedrops, he wondered.
他想问眼药水在哪。
He looked at the drugstore signs.
他看见了药店的标识。
Signs were hanging above each aisle.
每个过道上面都悬挂着标识。
He read sign after sign.
他挨个看了所有标识。
He finally saw the eyedrops sign.
他终于发现了眼药水的标识。
He walked to the eyedrops section.
他走向了眼药水柜台。
He looked on the top shelf.
他看了最上面的架子。
He looked on the middle shelf.
中间的架子。
He looked on the bottom shelf.
最底层的架子。
Where were the eyedrops?
眼药水在哪？
Was he in the right section?
他是在眼药水柜台吗？
He looked at the sign again.
他又看了看标识。
Yes, he was.
是的，他在。
He finally found the eyedrops.
他终于发现了眼药水。
He went to the front of the drugstore.
他走到了药店前面。
He stood in line to pay.
他排队等着付钱。
Someday, he thought, shopping will be faster.
他想总有一天购物将会变得更加便捷。
Someday, he thought, I will walk into this store.
他想有一天，我会走进这家商店。
I will ask out loud, "Where's the Crest toothpaste?"
大声叫嚷着“哪里有佳洁士牙膏”？
A bright light will appear above my head.
一盏明灯将出现在我头顶。
I will follow the light directly to the Crest toothpaste section.
我会跟着它径直走到佳洁士的柜台。
The light will disappear.
之后明灯消失。
My searching days will be over.
我的寻找之旅结束了。
