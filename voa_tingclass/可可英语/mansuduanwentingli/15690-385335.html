The phone rang as he was leaving the apartment.
他正要离开公寓时电话响了。
He didn't answer it.
他没有接。
It's probably Dave, he thought.
他想很有可能是戴夫。
His brother Dave had said he would call.
他的哥哥戴夫说要打电话。
He returned home an hour later.
一小时之后他回到家。
He checked his phone for missed calls.
他查看了未接电话。
There was only the one call.
只有一个未接电话。
But it wasn't his brother's number.
但是不是他哥哥戴夫的号码。
Who could it be, he wondered.
他想会是谁呢？
He dialed the number.
他打了这个电话。
A woman answered.
一位女士应答。
She said, "Hello, Kim, how are you doing?"
她说，“你好，金姆，怎么样了？
Kim was his wife's name.
金姆是他妻子的名字。
Who is this, he wondered.
他想这会是谁？
"Who's this?" he asked.
他问道，你是哪位？
"Who's this?" she asked. "You called me." He didn't answer.
她说，这是谁？你给我打电话。他没有接。
Now he realized who it was.
现在他知道是谁了。
It was some woman who called his wife occasionally.
偶尔会有女性给她的妻子打电话。
They had been classmates at school.
他们以前是同学。
His wife was polite to her.
他的妻子对她非常有礼貌。
But his wife didn't like her.
但是他的妻子并不喜欢她。
She never returned this woman's phone calls.
她从来没有回过她的电话。
He himself had never met or talked to her.
他也从来没有见过或是和她说过话。
"Sorry, wrong number," he said.
他说，“对不起，打错了。”
