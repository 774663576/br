The old man drove to Burger-Burger.
一名老人将车开到了汉堡店。
He was 82 years old.
他今年82岁。
It was late at night.
那天天色已晚。
It was 11:30 p.m.
那是晚上十一点半。
He was hungry.
他饥肠辘辘。
He wanted something to eat.
他想吃东西。
He went to the drive-through.
他将车开进了驾车服务窗口。
He ordered a Double Double Burger-Burger.
他点了一个双层汉堡。
He ordered large fries.
大薯条。
He ordered a large coffee.
大杯咖啡。
He drove his car forward.
他将车开向前方。
He gave the clerk $6.
他给了售货员6美金。
He waited.
他等了一会。
She gave him his Double Double Burger-Burger.
她给了她双层汉堡。
It smelled good.
汉堡闻起来很香。
He couldn't wait to eat it.
她等不及要吃。
She gave him his large fries.
她给了他大薯条。
They smelled good.
薯条闻起来很香。
He couldn't wait to eat them.
他迫不及待要吃。
She gave him his large cup of coffee.
她给了他大杯咖啡。
He couldn't wait to drink it.
他等不及要喝。
But the top wasn't on tight.
但是杯盖没有密封。
The hot coffee spilled.
热咖啡洒了。
It spilled into his lap.
咖啡洒到了膝盖上。
"Ouch!" he yelled. "Give me some ice!"
他叫嚷到：“噢！给我些冰！”
She gave him a large cup of ice.
她给了他一杯冰。
He poured it into his lap.
他将冰洒在了膝盖上。
"Ah," he said, "that feels better."
他说到：“嗯，感觉好多了。”
