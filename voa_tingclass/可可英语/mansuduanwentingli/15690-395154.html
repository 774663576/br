He lived in a one-bedroom apartment.
他住在一居室的楼房里。
He had lived there for a long time.
他已经住在这里很长时间了。
He usually went out on Sunday only.
他只有周日才会出来。
He took the bus to church.
他坐巴士去了教堂。
He took the bus to the supermarket.
他坐车去了超市。
He bought only canned food.
他只买罐头食物。
He bought canned tuna.
他买了金枪鱼罐头。
He bought canned chicken.
还买了鸡肉罐头。
He bought canned vegetables.
买了蔬菜罐头。
After shopping, he took the bus home.
购物之后，他坐车回了家。
He watched TV every day, all day long.
他每天都看电视，一看就是一天。
He threw his trash into plastic bags.
他把垃圾放到垃圾袋中。
His apartment was full of plastic bags.
他的房间充满了垃圾袋。
The plastic bags were full of empty cans.
垃圾袋中全是空罐头。
His son visited him occasionally.
他的儿子偶尔回来看他。
"Dad," he said, "you have to throw out all these bags.
他说：“爸爸，你必须把袋子全都拿出去。
They're a health hazard. You might get a disease. You might attract roaches.
它们会影响健康。你会生病的。这会招蟑螂。
They're a safety hazard. You might trip and fall down."
它们还有安全隐患。你也许会被绊倒，摔跤。”
His dad told him not to worry.
他的爸爸告诉他不要担心。
"I throw all these bags out as soon as they start to smell," he said.
他说：“等它们一变味我就扔出去。”
