The boys were going to the beach.
孩子们要去沙滩。
Dad was going to drive them to the beach.
爸爸开车送他们去沙滩。
The drive to the beach would take an hour.
需要一个小时车程。
Dave and Dick loved the beach.
戴夫和迪克喜欢沙滩。
They also loved the drive to the beach.
更喜欢开车去沙滩。
They loved to look out the window.
他们喜欢向窗外看。
They loved to stick their heads and arms out the window and feel the breeze.
喜欢将头和胳臂伸出窗外，感受迎风扑面的感觉。
They also loved to be in the front seat.
还喜欢坐在副驾驶。
They both preferred the front seat.
两人都想坐在副驾驶。
They both hated the back seat.
都讨厌坐在后座。
"Let's go, boys," Dad said. "Let's go to the beach."
爸爸说：“走吧，孩子们。去沙滩吧。”
Both boys ran out of the house.
孩子们跑出房间。
They ran straight to the front door of the car.
径直跑向车门前方。
Dave got there first.
戴夫先跑到。
Dick got there second.
迪克第二个。
Dave pulled the door open.
戴夫将车门打开。
Dick fell to the ground.
迪克摔了一跤。
Dave got into the front seat and shut the door.
戴夫坐上了副驾驶，关上了车门。
"Guess what? You can sit in the back seat," he said to Dick.
他对迪克说；“你要坐在后座了。”
