The passenger plane landed a year ago.
一年前，客机降落。
It was a famous landing.
这是一次成功的降落。
It was on TV and the radio.
此次降落上了电视和广播。
It was in the papers.
还登了报纸。
The plane landed in the Hudson River in January.
这架飞机是于一月份在哈德逊河降落的。
The pilot had no choice.
飞行员别无选择。
Both engines were damaged.
两个引擎都受到了损害。
He couldn't go back to the airport.
他无法飞回飞机场。
He couldn't land in New York City.
并无法降落在纽约城。
He had to land in the river.
他不得不在河中降落。
The plane landed gently.
飞机缓慢降落。
It was a soft landing.
这是一次软着落。
The plane didn't flip over.
飞机没有发生侧翻。
It landed on its belly.
机腹着陆。
No one died.
没有人死亡。
Everyone survived.
所有人成功获救。
Everyone got out of the plane.
所有人走出了飞机。
They stood on the wings of the plane.
他们站在机翼上。
Everyone was cold and wet.
所有人都感到寒冷，潮湿。
Boats rescued them all.
船只营救了他们。
It was the "Miracle on the Hudson."
这是一场“哈德逊奇迹”。
Today the same passengers got on two boats.
如今，当年的乘客乘坐两艘船。
The two boats went to where the plane had landed.
两艘船来到了当年飞机降落的地方。
They stopped on that spot in the Hudson.
他们停留在了那里。
Everyone hugged one another.
所有人热情相拥。
Life was good.
生活真美好。
