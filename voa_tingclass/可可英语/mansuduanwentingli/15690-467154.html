The three bears left their cave.
三只熊离开了它们的洞穴。
They lived in a beautiful cave.
它们住在一个漂亮的洞穴里。
The cave was big.
洞穴很大。
The cave was warm in winter.
冬天洞穴里很暖和。
The cave was cool in summer.
夏天洞穴里很凉快。
It was a quiet cave.
洞穴里很安静。
It was a dark cave.
洞穴里很黑暗。
They loved their cave.
它们很喜欢自己的洞穴。
Today was Sunday.
今天是周日。
They liked to pick berries on Sunday.
它们喜欢在周日去摘草莓。
So they left their cave.
所以它们离开了自己的洞穴。
They walked through the woods.
它们穿过丛林。
They found a field of berries.
它们找到了一片草莓地。
Mama bear had a basket.
熊妈妈有一个篮子。
They filled the basket with blackberries and blueberries.
它们把篮子里装满了黑莓和蓝莓。
Then they walked back to their cave.
然后它们回到了自己的洞穴。
When they got inside the cave, they heard a noise.
它们走进洞穴的时候，听到了一点声音。
"Who is that?" papa bear asked.
“谁在那儿？”熊爸爸问。
"It's only me," said a pig.
“是我，”一只小猪说。
"What are you doing here?" asked papa bear.
“你在这里干什么？”熊爸爸问。
"I'm hiding from the farmer," said the pig.
“我在躲避农民，”小猪说。
"I think he wants to eat me."
“我觉得他想吃了我。”
Papa bear told the pig he could stay in the cave as long as he wanted.
熊爸爸告诉小猪想在这里待多久都可以。
The bears shared their berries with the pig.
三只熊跟小猪一起分享草莓。
Then the pig and baby bear took a nap together.
然后小猪和熊宝宝一起睡午觉了。
