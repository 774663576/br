It's a painting of a seaside.
这是一张绘画海滨的画作。
The main colors are blue, white, and brown.
主要色是蓝色、白色以及棕色。
The sky is light blue.
天空是浅蓝色的。
A white cloud covers half the light blue sky.
白云占据了半个蓝天。
Two white birds fly through the air.
两只洁白的鸟在天空飞翔。
The bottom half of the painting is white sand and brown shrubs.
画作底部是白色的沙滩，棕色的灌木。
At the bottom right you can see part of the ocean.
在画面的右下角你可以看见部分海洋。
The ocean is dark blue with small white waves.
深蓝色的海洋和白色的海浪。
A boat is sailing by.
还有一只船驶过。
The boat has four big, white sails.
这艘船有4个巨型的白色风帆。
It has a wooden hull.
船身是木制的。
An old wooden house sits on the white sand.
白色沙滩上矗立着一件老旧的木屋。
No smoke comes from the brick chimney.
石砖烟囱中没有烟。
The roof sags in the middle.
房屋中间的屋顶往下塌落。
The house has a front porch.
屋前有门廊。
Five wooden posts support the porch.
五根木结构柱子支撑着门廊。
A clothesline is behind the house.
房屋后面有一条晒衣绳。
Clothes are hanging on the line.
衣服悬挂在绳子上。
But there are no people anywhere.
周围没有人。
Maybe they are inside the house.
或许他们在房子里。
Maybe they are walking along the beach.
或许他们在沙滩上散步。
