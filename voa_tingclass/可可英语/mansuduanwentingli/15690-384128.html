He was in his bedroom.
他在卧室。
He smelled food.
他闻到了食物的味道。
The food smelled like chicken.
闻起来像是鸡肉。
It smelled good.
味道很好闻。
A neighbor must be cooking dinner, he thought.
他想邻居一定是在做晚餐。
But how could he smell his neighbor's dinner?
但是他怎么能够闻到邻居家的晚餐呢？
The smell couldn't come through his apartment's heating vent.
这个味道一定不是从他家的通风口出来的。
That vent was for his apartment only.
通风口只供他们家用。
So where was the smell coming from?
那么味道是从哪里来的呢？
He walked to the front door.
他走到了前门。
He opened it.
打开了门。
There was no smell in the hallway outside the door.
门外的过道没有味道。
He walked into the living room.
他走进了客厅。
There was no smell in the living room.
客厅也没有味道。
He walked back into the bedroom.
他又回到了卧室。
He could smell the food again.
他又闻到了食物的味道。
The bedroom was the only place he could smell the food.
只有卧室能够闻到食物的味道。
It was winter. All the windows were closed.
现在是冬天。所有的窗户都是关着的。
How could the smell get into the bedroom?
味道是怎么进入卧室的呢？
"Call up the landlord," his wife said. "Maybe he knows how. I'm just glad we're smelling chicken. I'm glad it's not fish!"
他的妻子说，去叫房主人，或许他知道。幸好我们闻到的是鸡肉，不是鱼的味道。
