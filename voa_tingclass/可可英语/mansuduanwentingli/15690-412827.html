The garbage disposal was old.
垃圾处理器老化了。
It was leaking water.
它漏水了。
Eddie needed to replace it.
艾迪需要换新的。
He opened his toolbox.
他打开了工具箱。
He took a screwdriver out of his toolbox.
他从箱子里拿出了螺丝刀。
The garbage disposal had three screws.
垃圾处理器有三个螺丝。
He unscrewed the three screws.
他把螺丝拧了下来。
He took the garbage disposal out.
他将垃圾处理器拿了出来。
He put it on the kitchen floor.
将他放在厨房地面上。
He wanted to take it to the store.
他想把它带到商店。
He wanted to buy a new one at the store.
他想在商店买个新的。
But there was a problem.
但现在有个问题。
The garbage disposal was connected to a cable.
处理器与缆线相连接。
It was the electric cable.
这是电缆。
Eddie didn't know much about electricity.
艾迪对电没有多少了解。
He knew that electricity was dangerous.
他知道电很危险。
He knew that electricity could kill him.
他知道电能致命。
He put the garbage disposal back.
他把处理器放回了原位。
He screwed in the three screws.
他把三个螺丝重新拧上。
He put tape over the electric switch on the wall.
用胶条将墙上的电子开关粘上。
Now he could not turn on the garbage disposal.
现在处理器无法开启。
He called his landlord.
他叫了房东。
He said she needed to replace the garbage disposal.
他说她需要更换处理器了。
