This was his first visit to the corner library.
这是他第一次来到图书角。
He walked over to the library information desk.
他走到了图书馆信息台。
The woman behind the information desk was reading a magazine.
坐在信息台后面的女士正在阅读杂志。
He stood in front of her.
他站在她的前面。
She continued reading the magazine.
她仍然继续读杂志。
He cleared his throat.
他清了清嗓子。
She looked up from her magazine.
她抬起了头。
"I'm looking for a book," he said.
他说：“我在找一本书。”
"Title?" she asked.
她问道：“名字？”。
"'Good Manners, Bad Manners,'" he said.
他说：“有礼貌和无礼貌。”
She typed the title into her computer.
她把题目打进了电脑。
"Downstairs," she said.
她说：“在楼下”。
"How do I get downstairs?" he asked.
他问道：“怎么去楼下？”
She tilted her head to her right.
她的头向右倾了倾。
"The stairs are over there?" he asked.
他问道：“楼梯在那边吗？”
She tilted her head to her right again.
她的头又向右倾了倾。
"Is there a restroom downstairs, too?" he asked.
他问道：“楼下也有洗手间吗？”
She tilted her head to her left.
她把头向左倾了倾。
"The restroom is over there?" he asked.
他问道：“厕所在那边吗？”
She tilted her head to her left again.
她又把头向左倾了倾。
"Do you have a sore throat?" he asked.
他问道：“你是嗓子疼吗？”
She didn't answer.
她没有回答。
She was reading her magazine.
依然读着报纸。
