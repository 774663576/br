Paul called the 800 number.
保罗拨打了800。
He told them about his package.
他对他们说了自己的包裹。
His package was missing.
他的包裹丢失了。
"Where is my package?" he asked.
他问道：“我的包裹在哪？”
They told him it was in the warehouse.
他们告诉他在仓库。
They would deliver it tomorrow.
他们将在明天送达。
The next day the package didn't arrive.
第二天包裹没有送达。
He called the 800 number again.
他又拨打了800。
They told him it was still in the warehouse.
他们告诉他说包裹还在仓库。
They would deliver it tomorrow.
他们将在明天送达。
The next day he got a package.
第二天，他收到了包裹。
It was the wrong package.
包裹是错的。
He called the 800 number.
他拨打了800。
"Where is my package?" he asked.
他问道：“我的包裹在哪？”
They told him it was lost.
他们告诉他说丢了。
They couldn't find it.
他们找不到了。
They said they would look for it.
他们说要寻找包裹。
They told him to call back in a week.
他们告诉他在一周内打来。
He asked for a supervisor.
他询问了监察员。
He talked to a supervisor.
他告诉了监察员。
The supervisor said he would make a report.
监察员说他将报告此事。
"Will the report find my package?" Paul asked.
保罗问道；“报告会让我找到包裹吗？”
"No," said the supervisor. "But maybe your next package won't get lost."
监察员说：“不会，但是下回你的包裹将不会再丢失了。”
