Mama cat had six new kittens.
猫妈妈生了六只小猫。
Three kittens were black, and two were white.
三只黑的，两只白的。
One kitten was black, with white feet and a white face.
其中一只黑猫的脸和脚都是白的。
Nancy asked, "Mom, can we keep all the kittens?"
南希问道：“妈妈，我能认养所有小猫吗？”
Her mom said no.
妈妈说不行。
"We can't afford to keep six kittens," she said.
她说到：“我们没钱供养六只猫。
"When the kittens are three months old, we will give them away."
等小猫三个月大，我们就要把它们送人。”
Nancy asked if she could keep one kitten.
南希问是否能收养一只。
Her mom said okay.
妈妈说可以。
Nancy decided to keep the kitten with white feet.
南希决定收养那只脚是白色的小猫。
She called him Boots.
她管他叫Boots。
When the other five kittens were three months old, Nancy's mom took pictures of them.
等五只小猫三个月大时，南希的妈妈为他们拍摄了照片。
Nancy took the pictures to school.
南希将照片带进了学校。
She showed the pictures to her friends.
她向同学们展示图片。
All her friends wanted a kitten.
所有人都想收养一只。
They came to her house the next day.
第二天，他们来到了南希的家。
They took all the kittens except Boots.
除了小猫Boots，其它五只都被拿走了。
"Boots is my kitten," Nancy told her friends.
南希对朋友们说：“Boots是我的。”
Then her mom said, "We have to take mama cat to the cat doctor."
妈妈说：“我们要带猫妈妈去看病。”
Nancy asked why.
南希问为什么。
"We have to fix her," her mom said.
妈妈说；“我们要帮助她。
"We don't want her to have more kittens.
我们不能再让她生小猫了。
The doctor will fix her."
医生会帮助解决的。”
