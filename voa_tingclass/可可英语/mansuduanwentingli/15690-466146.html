The pilot and the passenger got into the plane.
飞行员和乘客上了飞机。
It was a small plane.
飞机很小。
It had one engine.
它有一个引擎。
It was an old plane.
它是一架旧飞机。
The pilot started the engine.
飞行员启动了引擎。
"Are you okay?" he asked the passenger.
“你还好吗？”他问乘客。
She said she was okay.
她说她很好。
They were flying to an island.
他们要飞往一座岛屿。
The island was only 30 minutes away.
去岛屿只要30分钟。
It would be a short flight.
飞行时间很短。
She was a nurse.
她是一名护士。
Her husband was a doctor.
她的丈夫是一名医生。
The doctor was on the island.
那名医生在岛上。
The doctor needed some medicine.
医生需要一些药物。
She had the medicine in her bag.
她的药物都装在包里。
The medicine would save a young boy.
这些药能救一个小男孩的生命。
The young boy was very sick.
那个小男孩病的很严重。
The plane took off.
飞机起飞了。
The engine sounded funny.
引擎的声音听起来很有趣。
The pilot frowned.
飞行员皱眉了。
"Is everything okay?" the passenger asked.
“没事吧？”乘客问道。
"I'm afraid not," said the pilot.
“估计有事，”飞行员说。
"There's something wrong with the engine. We have to land."
“引擎出问题了。我们得着陆。”
He landed the plane.
他把飞机降落了。
The nurse got out of the plane.
护士从飞机里面出来。
She looked around the airport.
她看了看机场周围。
She saw another plane.
她看到了另外一架飞机。
This plane had two engines.
这架飞机有两个发动机。
She walked over to the plane.
她走到飞机旁边。
"Is this a new plane or an old plane?" she asked the pilot.
“这是一家新飞机还是旧飞机？”她问飞行员。
The pilot said it was a new plane.
飞行员说这是一架新飞机。
"Good," she said.
“很好。”她说。
"Please take me to the island."
“请把我带到岛屿去。”
