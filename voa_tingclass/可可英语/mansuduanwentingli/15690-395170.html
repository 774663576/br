Something was wrong.
哪里出了问题。
Did he have a hernia again, in the same place?
他是不是又在同一个地方患了疝气？
Thirty years ago he had a hernia.
30年前他患了疝气。
A doctor had repaired the hernia.
医生帮他进行了治疗。
Now he felt some pain.
如今，他又感到了疼痛。
It was below his navel.
疼痛感在肚脐的下部。
It was to the left of his navel.
在肚脐的左面。
It wasn't a sharp pain.
这种疼不是刺痛。
It was a dull ache.
而是隐隐作痛。
It had started about two months ago.
疼痛开始于2个月前。
Now it was getting worse.
现在变得更加严重了。
Now it was a constant ache.
现在是持续疼痛。
He called a few doctors.
他叫了几名医生。
"How much is a consult?" he asked.
他问道：“咨询费是多少？”
One doctor said $300.
一名医生说300美金。
Another doctor said $350.
另一名说350美金。
That's a lot of money, he thought.
他想这么多钱。
Maybe this ache will go away, he thought.
他想疼痛会过去的。
Maybe it'll go away by itself.
或许它会痊愈的。
He decided to wait another week.
他决定再等一周。
Then I'll make an appointment, he thought.
之后再进行预约。
I'll spend the money.
我会花钱。
It's a lot of money.
花很多的钱。
But it's cheaper to fix a small problem now than a big problem later.
但是现在花钱看小病总比将来看大病要便宜。
