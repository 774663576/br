Ray called up the newspaper reporter.
雷给新闻报记者打电话。
"You wrote about a bank robbery. You described the bank robber," he said. "Why did you describe him?"
他说：“你们报道了抢劫银行一案。你们对劫匪进行了描述。你们为什么要对他进行描述？”
The reporter said, "We want the public to help find him.
记者称：“我们希望能够通过社会来找寻他。
If someone sees him, they can call the police."
如果有人看见了他，他们就能够报警。”
Ray said, "But why didn't you tell us his race?
雷称：“但是你们为什么不告诉我他的种族？
You said he was about 35 and about 5 feet 7 inches.
你们说他大约有35岁，个头约为五尺七寸。
But you didn't tell us his race.
但是你们并没有提及他的种族。
That would help a lot."
那将会非常有帮助。
"Our lawyers say we can't. It's racist," said the reporter.
记者称：“我们的律师说不能。那是种族主义者。
"The robber can sue us for being racist."
劫匪能够状告我们种族偏见。
"Well, don't report his age, either," said Ray.
雷说：“那么也别报道他的年龄了。
"That's ageist.
那是年龄歧视。
Don't report his height.
别报道他的身高了。
That's 'heightist.'
那是身高歧视。
Don't report his sex.
还有性别。
That's sexist."
那是性别歧视。
"That doesn't leave much to report," said the reporter.
记者称：“没有什么可以报道的了。”
"Just report this," said Ray. "'A human being robbed a bank.
雷说：“只报道这个。有人抢劫银行。
Please look for a human being who looks like he or she robbed a bank.
请注意那些看上去像是抢过银行的他或她。
