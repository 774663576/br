"I hear something," Ben told the doctor.
本告诉医生：“我总是能听到声音”。
"What do you hear?" asked the doctor.
医生问本：“你听到了什么？”
Ben heard a buzz.
本听到了嗡嗡声。
The buzz sounded like a mosquito.
有点像蚊子的嗡嗡声。
It sounded like a mosquito was in his ear.
蚊子似乎就在他的耳朵里。
"Which ear?" asked the doctor.
医生问道：“哪只耳朵？”
"Both ears," said Ben.
本说：“两只耳朵。”
"Do you hear a buzz all the time?" the doctor asked.
医生问道：“你总是能听到嗡嗡声吗？”
"Yes, I hear a buzz all the time," Ben said.
本说：：是的，我总是能听到。”
The doctor looked in Ben's left ear.
医生检查了本的左耳。
The doctor looked in Ben's right ear.
又看了右耳。
"Are any mosquitoes in my ears?" asked Ben.
本问道：“我的耳朵里有蚊子吗？”
"No," said the doctor, "no mosquitoes are in your ears."
医生说：“没有，耳朵里没有蚊子。”
Ben asked, "So, why do I hear a buzz in my ears?"
本问道；“为什么我总能听到嗡嗡声？”
"You have tinnitus," said the doctor.
医生说：“你得了耳鸣。”
"Tinnitus? What's that?" Ben asked.
本问道：“耳鸣？这是什么？”
The doctor said tinnitus is a buzzing sound.
医生说患有耳鸣就会产生嗡嗡声。
"Can you fix it?" Ben asked.
本问道：“你能治愈吗？”
"No, I cannot fix it. You have to live with it," said the doctor.
医生说：“无法治愈，你只能这样了。”
