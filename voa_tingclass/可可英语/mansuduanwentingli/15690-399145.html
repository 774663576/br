He opened the envelope.
他打开了信封。
It contained junk mail.
里面有一封垃圾邮件。
The junk mail was an ad.
这封垃圾邮件是一则广告。
It was an ad for hospital insurance.
广告是关于医疗保险的。
Did he want extra hospital insurance?
他是否希望拥有医疗保险？
He didn't have any hospital insurance.
他并没有医疗保险。
Did he want extra money to help pay hospital bills?
他是否需要额外资金来帮助他支付医疗费用？
Yes, he thought, extra money would be nice.
他想是的，额外资金很不错。
He read on.
他读了起来。
The ad said the average hospital stay in New York is seven days.
广告称纽约医院病患的平均住院天数为7天。
It said the average cost per day in New York is $3,000.
在纽约，每天的平均开销是3000美金。
I should move to a cheaper state, he thought.
他想他应该搬到消费水平不高的大州。
But he couldn't afford to move.
但是他没钱搬家。
He read on.
他又继续往下读。
It said the insurance was only $15 a month.
广告称这份保险每个月仅为15美金。
That's cheap, he thought.
他想这很便宜。
It said the insurance pays $50 a day.
广告还说每天的保险为50美金。
He laughed.
他笑了起来。
My hospital bill will be $3,000 a day.
我每天的医疗费用为3000美金。
My extra insurance will pay $50 a day.
我的保险每天会支付50美金。
What good is that, he thought.
他想这有什么用。
What good is $50 a day?
每天50美金有什么用？
