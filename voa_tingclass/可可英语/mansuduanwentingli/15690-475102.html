The farmer drives a tractor.
农民开着拖拉机。
The tractor digs up the ground.
拖拉机挖着地面。
He plants yellow corn in the ground.
他往地里种黄玉米。
He plants the yellow corn in the spring.
他在春天种黄玉米。
The corn grows in the summer.
玉米在夏季生长。
The rain helps the corn grow.
雨水有助于玉米生长。
If there is no rain, the corn dies.
如果不下雨，玉米就会死掉。
If there is a lot of rain, there is a lot of corn.
如果降雨很多，玉米就会有很多。
He harvests the yellow corn in late summer.
他在夏末的时候收黄玉米。
He sells the corn at his vegetable stand.
他在自己的菜铺上卖玉米。
He sells one ear for 25 cents.
每根玉米25美分。
He sells four ears for $1.
四根玉米1美元。
He sells all his corn in just one month.
他在一个月内就能卖完所有玉米。
The neighbors love his corn.
邻居们很喜欢他的玉米。
The corn is fresh.
玉米很新鲜。
It is bright yellow.
是嫩黄色的。
It is tasty. It is delicious.
很好吃。很美味。
The birds love his corn, too.
鸟儿们也很喜欢他的玉米。
They don't pay for it.
它们吃是免费的。
They eat it while it is in the field.
玉米还在地里的时候鸟儿们会去吃。
They don't cook it.
它们不用把玉米做熟。
They eat it raw.
它们直接生吃。
The farmer doesn't get angry.
农民并不生气。
Birds have to eat, too.
鸟儿们也要吃东西。
So do cows. The farmer has one cow.
奶牛也是。农民有一头奶牛。
He gives his cow fresh corn every summer.
他每年夏天都会给奶牛吃新鲜的玉米。
The cow loves the corn.
奶牛很喜欢玉米。
It eats a lot of yellow corn in the summer.
它在夏天会吃很多黄玉米。
Sometimes it makes yellow milk.
有时候它会产黄色的牛奶。
