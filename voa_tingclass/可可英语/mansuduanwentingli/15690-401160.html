It was January in Manhattan.
这是曼哈顿的一月。
It was a mild, sunny day.
这天阳光明媚，气候温和。
A man stood at 42nd Street and Broadway.
一名男子站在42号百老汇大街上。
His blond hair hung down to his broad shoulders.
金黄色的头发散落在他的宽肩膀上。
He was wearing a cowboy hat.
他头戴牛仔帽。
He was wearing cowboy boots.
脚穿牛仔靴。
He was wearing tight, white underwear.
他穿了一件紧身的白色内衣。
He wasn't wearing a shirt.
他没有穿T恤。
He wasn't wearing pants.
也没有穿裤子。
Written on the back of his white underwear was "The Naked Cowboy."
白色内衣后面写着“赤裸的牛仔。”
He was playing his guitar.
他在弹吉他。
People stopped to look at him.
人们停下来看他。
A young couple looked at him.
一对年轻的夫妻看着他。
The woman was laughing.
女孩开怀大笑。
She said something to her boyfriend.
她对男朋友说了什么。
She said something to the cowboy.
她又对牛仔说了什么。
He waved her over.
他招手让她过来。
She walked over to him.
她走向他。
He put his muscular arm around her.
他将强壮有力的胳臂放在了她肩上。
Her boyfriend took a picture.
她的男朋友照了张相。
She gave the cowboy a $5 bill.
她给了牛仔5美元。
He put it into his guitar.
他将钱放进了吉他里。
Another woman walked over to him.
另一个女孩走向了他。
He put his arm around her.
他将胳臂搭在她肩上。
Her friend took their picture.
她的朋友为他们拍了照。
