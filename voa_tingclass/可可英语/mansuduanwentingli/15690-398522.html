His apartment is on the seventh floor.
他的公寓在7层。
He can see the busy street from his bedroom window.
他从卧室的窗户能够看到繁忙的大街。
The sidewalk is often crowded with people.
人行道上总是挤满了行人。
The sidewalk is wide.
人行道很宽。
It's about 20 feet wide.
有大约20尺宽。
New York City has many wide sidewalks.
纽约城有许多宽广的人行道。
He can see several stores.
他能够看到许多商店。
One store is a clothing store.
一家是服装店。
Two stores are shoe stores.
还有两家鞋店。
A nearby building has a shiny silver smokestack.
旁边的建筑有一个闪闪发亮的银色烟囱。
It's a tall, skinny smokestack.
这是一个高耸的细长烟囱。
It's 15 stories tall.
有15层楼高。
Pigeons fly above the street.
鸽子从大街上飞翔而过。
They land on building ledges.
它们停在了建筑的壁架上。
The ledges are beneath the windows.
壁架在窗户下面。
Two or three pigeons land on the same ledge.
两三只鸽子停在一个壁架上。
They sit there for a while.
它们会停一会。
Then they fly away.
之后飞走了。
Yesterday he saw a red balloon.
昨天他看见了一个红色气球。
It was as big as a soccer ball.
它和足球一样大。
It rose to his eye level.
气球飞到了与他眼睛持平的高度。
It quickly rose higher.
之后迅速上升。
In seconds it was gone.
转眼间就不见了。
Where is it today?
它今天在哪？
It might be floating over the cold, blue Atlantic.
它或许正在寒冷，蔚蓝的大西洋上飞行。
