The children were hungry.
孩子们很饿。
They looked out the window.
他们看着窗户外面。
Where was their mother?
他们的妈妈在哪儿呢？
She walked into the house.
她走进屋里。
The children ran over to her.
孩子们朝她跑去。
"Mama, we're so hungry," they both said.
“妈妈，我们好饿。”他们说。
She said lunch was coming.
她说午饭一会儿就好。
She walked into the kitchen.
她走进了厨房。
She opened a can of chicken soup.
她打开了一罐鸡汤。
She poured the soup into a pot.
她把汤倒进锅里。
She added water.
她加了水。
She put the pot on the stove.
她把锅放在炉子上。
She made two peanut butter and jelly sandwiches.
她做了两个花生黄油果冻三明治。
She sliced an apple.
她切了一个苹果。
The soup was hot.
汤很热。
She poured it into two bowls.
她把汤倒进两个碗里。
She put the sandwiches on two plates.
她把三明治放在两个盘子里。
She put apple slices on each plate.
她把苹果分别放在每个盘子里。
She put the bowls and plates on the table.
她把碗和盘子放在餐桌上。
The children ran to the table.
孩子们跑到餐桌旁边。
"Thank you, mommy!" they said.
“谢谢你，妈妈！”他们说。
Then they started eating.
然后他们开始吃饭了。
The cat and the dog watched them eat.
猫和狗看着他们吃饭。
