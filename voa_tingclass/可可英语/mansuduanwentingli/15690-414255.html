"Where is the mustard?" asked a prisoner.
犯人问道：“芥末酱在哪？”
"There is no mustard," said the cook.
厨师说：“没有芥末酱，
"We ran out of mustard. We have no more mustard.
芥末酱吃完了，没有剩余了。
We'll get more mustard tomorrow."
明天会有更多的芥末酱。”
The prisoner looked at his hot dog.
犯人看着他的热狗。
A hot dog without mustard is not a hot dog.
没有芥末酱的热狗不是热狗。
"I can't eat this hot dog without mustard!" he yelled.
他叫嚷到：“没有芥末酱我无法下咽！”
"A hot dog without mustard is not a hot dog.
没有芥末酱的热狗不是热狗。
I want some mustard!"
我要芥末酱！
The other prisoners heard him yelling.
其他犯人听到了他的叫声。
They started yelling, too.
他们也开始叫嚷起来。
"We want mustard! We want mustard!"
我们要芥末酱。要芥末酱！
The cook said, "Shut up! Eat your hot dogs!
厨师说：“闭嘴！吃你们的热狗！
We have plenty of mayonnaise.
我们有许多蛋黄酱。
Put mayonnaise on your hot dogs."
在热狗上涂抹蛋黄酱。”
The prisoners were angry.
犯人非常生气。
"Don't tell us to shut up!" they yelled.
他们叫嚷到：“不要叫我们闭嘴。
They threw their hot dogs at the cook.
他们将热狗扔给了厨师。
They threw their hot dogs at the guards.
他们将热狗扔给了警卫。
They threw their hot dogs at one another.
扔给了对方。

将没有芥末酱的热狗乱扔一气。
