It was Sunday in New York City.
纽约城，周日的一天。
He needed some cash.
他需要现金。
He walked to the bank on the corner.
他走向了拐角的银行。
Next to the bank were six indoor ATMs.
银行旁边有6台室内自动取款机。
A woman was using one of the ATMs.
一名妇女正在使用其中一台。
He pulled on the glass door.
他拉开了玻璃门。
The door was locked.
门上了锁。
He pulled again.
他又拉了一次。
How did she get in there?
她是怎么进去的？
She finished using the ATM.
她从自动取款机前离开。
She walked toward him.
她走向他。
She opened the door.
她打开了门。
She stepped outside.
她走了出去。
He held onto the door.
他扶住了门。
He walked inside.
他走了进去。
She must have waited for someone, too, he thought.
他想，她一定要等个人过来。
He put his card into the ATM.
他将卡放进了自动取款机。
He got his cash.
他取出了现金。
He started to walk out.
他开始向外走。
Just then a young woman walked in.
就在这时，一位年轻的女性向里走。
He couldn't believe it. "Excuse me," he said. "How did you get in here? That door is locked."
他不敢相信。他问道：“对不起，你是怎么进去的？门是上锁的。”
"Outside there's a slot next to the door," she said. "Just slide your ATM card into the slot. The door will unlock."
她说：“旁边门上有一个放卡的地方。将卡在这里一刷。门就不会锁上了。”
