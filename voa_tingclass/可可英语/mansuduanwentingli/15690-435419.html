Jimmy wanted to cross the street.
吉米想过马路。
He was on the south side of the street.
他站在马路的南面。
He wanted to get to the north side of the street.
想去马路的北面。
He was in the middle of the block.
他位于街区中间。
His father always told him to cross at an intersection.
父亲告诉他在十字路口过马路。
His father always said never cross in the middle of the block.
不要在街区中间过马路。
Jimmy walked to the intersection.
吉米走到十字路口。
There was a traffic light at the intersection.
那里设有指示灯。
It had three colors red, green, and yellow.
有红绿黄三种颜色。
Red was at the top.
红色在上面。
Green was at the bottom.
绿色在下面。
Yellow was in the middle.
黄色在中间。
He looked at the crosswalk sign.
他看到了人行横道指示灯。
It was red.
指示灯是红色。
It was a red hand.
是一个大大的红手。
The red hand meant Don't Walk.
“红手”意思是禁止通行。
He waited for the red hand to change.
他等待“红手”变灯。
It changed to a white figure walking.
指示灯变成了“小白人行走”。
The white figure walking meant Walk.
“小白人行走”的意思是可以通行。
Jimmy looked both ways.
吉米向左右看了看。
Then he walked across the street.
他通过了马路。
He walked in the crosswalk.
他在人行横道通过了马路。
