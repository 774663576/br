He walked into the doctor's office.
他走进医生办公室。
The receptionist said hello.
前台向他问好。
He said hello.
他也向她问好。
She asked if he had an appointment.
她向他询问是否有约。
He said, "Yes, I have an appointment at 2 o'clock."
他说：“我两点有约。”
She told him to sign in.
她让他签字。
He signed in.
他签了字。
"Which doctor are you seeing?" she asked.
她询问到：“你预约的是哪位医生？”
"I'm seeing Dr. Evans," he said.
他说：“是埃文斯医生。”
She told him to take a seat.
她让他坐下等待。
Dr. Evans would see him in a few minutes.
埃文斯医生随后就到。
He took a seat.
他坐了下来。
He opened a magazine.
他看杂志。
The name of the magazine was Time.
杂志名称是“时代”。
He looked at the date on the magazine.
他看了看杂志日期。
The date was November 17, 2008.
日期是2008年12月17日。
The magazine was one year old.
杂志已经发刊一年。
He didn't mind.
他不介意。
Old news was interesting.
旧新闻更有趣。
New news was interesting.
时事新闻也有趣。
He read the old news.
他开始阅读旧新闻。
It was about a new president.
新闻是关于新任总统。
Barack Obama was the new president.
巴拉克·奥巴马为新总统。
He was the new president of the United States.
他是美国新总统。
Everyone was so happy.
所有人欢呼雀跃。
Finally, a black man was president.
黑人终于成为了总统。
