Emily sat down on the sofa.
艾米丽坐在沙发上。
She took off her shoes.
她脱掉鞋。
She put her feet on the coffee table.
将脚放在咖啡桌上。
She leaned back.
并向后仰。
She picked up the remote and turned on the TV.
拿起遥控器，打开电视。
It was 6 o'clock.
现在是晚上六点。
The news was on three channels.
有三个频道转播新闻。
She didn't want to see the news.
她不想听新闻。
The news depressed her.
新闻让她感到不悦。
It was usually about accidents and murders.
新闻通常与事故谋杀有关。
There was too much bad news on the TV news.
新闻中尽是坏消息。
She found a talk show to watch.
她找到了一个脱口秀节目。
It was Ellen.
主持人是艾伦。
She liked Ellen.
她喜欢艾伦。
Ellen was a comedian.
艾伦是一个喜剧演员。
She told good jokes.
她会讲笑话。
She showed animals doing funny things.
向人们展示会搞笑的动物。
She gave expensive gifts to her fans occasionally.
偶尔向粉丝赠送贵重礼物。
This show was a rerun, because Ellen's "live" show was usually on at 4 o'clock.
现在是重播，因为艾伦的现场直播通常会在下午四点。
But that didn't matter to Emily.
但艾米丽却不在乎。
All she wanted to do was relax.
她想做的只有放松。
Her life was one problem after another.
她的生活是一个麻烦接着另一个麻烦。
Her husband had just lost his job.
丈夫刚刚丢了工作。
Her only child had dropped out of high school.
她唯一的孩子高中辍学。
Even worse, he had left home.
更严重的是，他离开了家。
He told his mom he would call from time to time.
对艾米丽说他会时常打电话过来。
But he hadn't called in two weeks.
但他两周都没打电话了。
She was worried sick.
她因为担心而生了病。
She didn't know where he was.
她不知道他在哪。
She didn't know if he was dead or alive.
不知道他是死是活。
Her husband said she shouldn't worry.
丈夫说不要担心。
He had done the same thing when he was 17.
他17岁时也是这么干的。
But when he found out that life on the road wasn't as easy as he thought it was, he returned home and got his high school diploma.
但当他发现生活远比想象的复杂时，他回了家，完成了高中学业。
