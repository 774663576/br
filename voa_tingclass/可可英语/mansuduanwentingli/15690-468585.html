小女孩很渴。
She wanted something to drink.
她想喝点东西。
She opened the refrigerator.
她打开了冰箱。
She looked inside.
她往里面看了看。
She saw milk.
她看到了牛奶。
She saw soda.
她看到了苏打水。
She saw orange juice.
她看到了橙汁。
She saw beer.
她看到了啤酒。
She wondered what beer tasted like.
她想知道啤酒是什么味道。
Her daddy drank beer every day.
她爸爸每天都喝啤酒。
Beer must be good.
啤酒一定很好喝。
If Daddy likes it, it must taste good.
如果爸爸喜欢喝，那它肯定很好喝。
She grabbed a can of beer.
她拿起一罐啤酒。
She opened the beer.
她把啤酒打开了。
She smelled it.
她闻了闻。
She couldn't smell anything.
她什么也闻不到。
She swallowed some.
她喝了一口。

“呸！”她说。

啤酒太难喝了。
How could Daddy drink that stuff?
爸爸怎么会喝这种东西呢？
She put the can back into the refrigerator.
她把啤酒罐放回冰箱里了。
Daddy could finish it when he got home.
爸爸回家以后可以把它喝完。
She grabbed the milk.
她拿起了牛奶。
She poured some into a glass.
她倒了一些牛奶在杯子里。
She added chocolate syrup to the milk.
她往牛奶里加了一些巧克力糖浆。
She stirred the milk.
她搅了搅牛奶。
Now she had chocolate milk.
现在她有了巧克力牛奶。
She loved chocolate milk.
她喜欢巧克力牛奶。
Poor Daddy, she thought.
爸爸真可怜，她这样想着。
He likes beer more than chocolate milk.
他喜欢啤酒超过巧克力牛奶。
