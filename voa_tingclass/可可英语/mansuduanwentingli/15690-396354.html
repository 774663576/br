Edie's birthday was today.
今天是艾迪的生日。
"I want nothing for my birthday," she had said.
她曾说她生日什么都不想要。
Nick knew better.
尼克知道。
He knew that "nothing" meant "something."
什么都不想要的意思是希望收到礼物。
He went to the bakery in the morning.
他早上去了蛋糕店。
He looked at the cakes.
他看着这些蛋糕。
She liked cake without icing.
她喜欢没有糖衣的蛋糕。
He looked for a cake without icing.
他正在找寻这种蛋糕。
He didn't see one.
他没有发现有这样的蛋糕。
But he did see a carrot cake.
但是他发现了一个胡萝卜蛋糕。
The cake had vanilla icing.
上面有香草味糖衣。
He liked vanilla icing.
他喜欢香草味糖衣。
She liked carrot cake.
而她喜欢胡萝卜蛋糕。
He bought the cake.
他买了这个蛋糕。
The baker wrote "Happy Birthday Edie" on the cake.
面包师在蛋糕上写下了“艾迪，生日快乐。”
She'll like this cake, Nick thought.
尼克想，她会喜欢的。
He took it home.
他带回了家。
He showed Edie the cake.
他向艾迪展示了蛋糕。
"It looks delicious," she said.
她说，看起来非常美味。
"But you have to eat all the icing.
但是你要吃掉所有的糖衣。
Did you buy this cake for me or for you?
这个蛋糕是为你买的还是为我买的？
I know you love icing.
我知道你喜欢糖衣。
You know I don't like icing.
你知道我不喜欢这个。
Next year buy me a cake without icing.
明年给我买一个不带糖衣的蛋糕。
You have a whole year to find one."
你有一整年的时间去寻找。
