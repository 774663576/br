Jenny went to the lake.
詹妮去了湖边。
She went to the lake with her dad.
她是和父亲一起去的。
It was a big lake.
湖面很宽。
It was a big, blue lake.
湖面很宽，湖水很蓝。
It had a lot of water in it.
湖面波涛。
It had a lot of fish in it.
鱼儿们生活在这里。
Big fish were in the lake.
有大鱼。
Little fish were in the lake.
有小鱼。
Jenny didn't see any fish.
詹妮没看到鱼。
"I don't see any fish. Where are the fish, Daddy?" she asked.
她问道：“我没看到鱼，爸爸鱼在哪？”
"They're swimming around. They're looking for food," her dad said.
爸爸对她说：“它们正在四处游荡，找寻食物。”
Dad put a worm on a hook.
父亲将虫子放在鱼钩上。
"Does the hook hurt the worm?" Jenny asked.
詹妮问道：“鱼钩会弄疼虫子吗？”
"No, the hook doesn't hurt the worm," her dad said.
爸爸说；“它们不会。”
"How do you know?" she asked.
她问道：“你怎么知道？”
"How do I know?" her dad asked.
爸爸问道：“我怎么知道？”
"Well, the worm doesn't say anything. So I guess the hook doesn't hurt it."
父亲回答道：“虫子什么都没说。我想鱼钩不会弄疼它。”
Jenny said, "But how can the worm say anything, Daddy? The worm can't talk!"
詹妮说：“虫子怎么会说话？它们不会讲话！”
