Barbara likes funny stories.
芭芭拉喜欢笑话。
She likes to hear funny stories.
她喜欢听笑话。
She likes to tell funny stories.
她喜欢讲笑话。
She told her mom a funny story.
她给妈妈讲了一个笑话。
When she finished, she waited for her mom to laugh.
当她讲完后，一直等着妈妈大笑。
"Mom, why aren't you laughing? That was a funny story," Barbara said.
“妈妈，为什么你不笑？那个笑话很搞笑，”芭芭拉说。
"Oh, I'm sorry," her mom said.
“哦，对不起，”妈妈说。
"Sometimes you think something is funny, but someone else thinks it isn't funny."
“有时你觉得一些事情很好笑，但是别人不一定认为它很好笑。”
So, Barbara's mom did not laugh at Barbara's story.
所以，芭芭拉的妈妈听完笑话后没有大笑。
Barbara told the same story to her younger sister.
芭芭拉把同样的故事讲给她妹妹听。
Her younger sister laughed at the story.
妹妹听到故事后笑了起来。
