Sandra wrote a check to her gas company.
桑德拉给她的煤气公司写了张支票。
On one line she wrote "May 17, 2009."
一行写着“2009年5月17日。”
On the next line she wrote "The Gas Company."
下一行她写上“煤气公司。”
On another line she wrote "$35.66."
另一行她写上“35.66美元。”
On another line she wrote "Thirty-five and 66/100."
另一行她写上“35又100分之66.”
On another line she wrote "Monthly Gas Bill."
另一行她写上“每月汽油钱。”
On another line she signed her name.
另一行她签上了自己的名字。
Then she put the check into an envelope.
然后她把这张支票放到了一个信封里。
She also put the gas bill into the envelope.
她也把汽油钱放到了信封里。
She licked the envelope and sealed it.
她舔舔信封并封住口。
She put her return address on the front of the envelope.
她把回信地址卸载信封正面。
She put a 44-cent stamp on the front of the envelope.
她在信封正面贴了一张44美分的邮票。
She put the envelope in the mailbox.
她把信封放进了信箱。
