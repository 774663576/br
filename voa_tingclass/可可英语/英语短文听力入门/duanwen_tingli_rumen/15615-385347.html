Emma went to the market.
艾玛去了市场。
She needed to buy food.
她需要买些食物。
She needed brown eggs.
她需要棕色鸡蛋。
She opened a carton of brown eggs.
她打开一箱棕色鸡蛋。
She looked at the eggs.
她看看这些鸡蛋。
None of them were cracked.
没有一个鸡蛋是破的。
None of them were broken.
没有一个鸡蛋被打破。
All the eggs were okay.
所有鸡蛋都很好。
She closed the carton.
她盖上箱子。
She put the carton of eggs in her shopping cart.
她把这箱鸡蛋放进了购物车。
She needed red apples.
她需要买些红苹果。
She looked at a bag of red apples.
她看了一袋红苹果。
She looked for dark spots on the apples.
她在苹果上找黑点。
She looked for worm holes in the apples.
她在苹果上找虫眼。
She didn't see any dark spots or worm holes.
她没看到有任何黑点或者虫眼。

她把这袋苹果放进了购物车。
