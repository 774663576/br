Many homeless people were sitting on the sidewalk.
许多无家可归的人坐在人行道上。
A young man walked up to each homeless person.
一个年轻人走向每一个无家可归的人。
He gave each person a flier.
他给了每个人一个传单。
The flier was from a trade school.
这传单来自一所贸易学校。
The flier described a class.
这传单描述一个课程。
The trade school was offering a barber class.
这所贸易学校提供一个理发师的培训班。
The class was free for homeless people.
这个课程对无家可归的人是免费的。
Matt read the flier. He talked to the man.
马特读了传单，他走向这个人。
Matt said he wanted to take the class.
马特说他想去参加这个课程。
The man wrote down Matt's name.
这个人写下了马特的姓名。
He told Matt to go to the school on Monday.
他告诉马特周一去学校。
"How long does the class last?" Matt asked.
“这个课程持续多长时间？”马特问。
"The class lasts eight weeks," the man said.
“这个课程持续八周，”这人说。
"Will the school find me a job?" Matt asked.
“这学校能给我找到一个工作吗？”马特问。
"No, you have to find your own job," the man said.
“不能，有必须自己去找工作，”这人说。
"We can only do so much."
“我们能做的只有这么多。”
