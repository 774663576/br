Charles found a glass bottle.
查尔斯发现了一个玻璃瓶。
He found the glass bottle in his back yard.
他在自家后院发现了一个玻璃瓶。
It was a pretty glass bottle.
玻璃瓶很漂亮。
It was dark green.
它是深绿色的。
He looked inside the dark green bottle.
他朝深绿色玻璃瓶的里面看了看。
He couldn't see anything.
他看不到任何东西。
He shook the bottle.
他摇摇瓶子。
Something came out of the bottle.
有个东西从瓶子里掉出来。
It landed on the ground.
它掉到地上。
It was a bug.
那是个臭虫。
Charles picked up the bug.
查尔斯捡起臭虫。
He looked at it.
他看着它。
The bug looked at Charles.
臭虫看着查尔斯。
Charles put the bottle back on the ground.
查尔斯把瓶子放回地上。
He put the bug on the ground, next to the bottle.
他把臭虫放到地上，挨着瓶子。
The bug crawled back into the bottle.
臭虫爬回了瓶子。
