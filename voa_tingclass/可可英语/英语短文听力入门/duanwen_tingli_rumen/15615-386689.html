Fay went into the bathroom.
费伊走进浴室。
She turned on the cold water.
她把冷水打开。
She turned on the hot water.
她把热水打开。
Warm water came out of the faucet.
温水从水龙头里流出来。
She put her hands under the warm water.
她把手放在温水下。
She rubbed her hands together.
她搓搓双手。
She picked up a bar of white soap.
她拿起一块白色香皂。
She rubbed the soap with her hands.
她用手搓搓香皂。
She put the soap back.
她把香皂放回去。
She washed her hands for half a minute.
她洗了半分钟手。
Then she rinsed her hands with the water.
然后她用水冲冲手。
She turned off the hot water.
她把热水关掉。
She turned off the cold water.
她把冷水关掉。
She dried her hands with a towel.
她用毛巾擦干手。
