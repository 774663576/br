William looked at his bowl of soup.
威廉姆看看他碗里的汤。
It was chicken soup.
那是鸡汤。
There was white rice in the soup.
汤里有白米饭。
William liked rice.
威廉姆爱吃米饭。
There were pieces of chicken in the soup.
汤里有几块鸡肉。
William loved chicken.
威廉姆爱吃鸡肉。
The pieces of chicken were white.
这几块鸡肉是白色的。
They were small and square.
它们小小的，方方的。
They weren't big and round.
它们不大也不圆。
There were pieces of orange carrots in the soup.
汤里有橙色胡萝卜。
William didn't like carrots.
威廉姆不喜欢吃胡萝卜。
He picked up a piece of carrot with his fingers.
他用手拣出来一块胡萝卜。
He put it on the table next to his bowl.
他把它放到桌子上的碗旁边。
He took all the pieces of carrots out of the bowl.
他把所有的胡萝卜都拣了出来。
He put them all on the table.
他把它们都放到桌子上。
Then he ate his soup.
然后他开吃喝汤。
