He dropped his toothbrush.
他掉了他的牙刷。
It fell on the floor.
它摔在地板上。
He picked up his toothbrush.
他拿起牙刷。
He rinsed it off.
他冲洗它。
He brushed his teeth.
他刷了牙。
He rinsed his mouth.
他清洗他的嘴。
He walked out of the bathroom.
他走出浴室。
He sat on his bed.
他坐在自己的床上。
He felt something.
他感觉到了什么。
It was under the sheet.
它在被单下面。
He took the sheet off the bed.
他把被单从床上拿开。
Something was in his mattress.
有什么东西在他的床垫里。
It was a brand new mattress.
这是一个全新的床垫。
He had bought it yesterday.
他昨天买的它。
What is in the mattress, he wondered.
什么在床垫里，他不知道。
He went to the kitchen.
他去了厨房。
He took a knife from the kitchen drawer.
他从厨房的抽屉里拿了一把刀。
He cut open the mattress.
他剖开的床垫。
What could it be, he wondered.
可能是什么，他在想。
It was small. It was black.
它是小的。它是黑色的。
It was a book.
这是一本书。
It was the New Testament.
它是新约全书。
Is God trying to tell me something, he wondered.
是上帝想告诉我什么，他在想。
