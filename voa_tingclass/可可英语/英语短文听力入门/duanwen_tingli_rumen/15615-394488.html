They walked onto the dock.
他们走上码头。
They got into the boat.
他们走上船。
They had all their fishing gear.
他们准备好了所有的钓鱼工具。
They were going fishing.
他们要去钓鱼。
They loved to go fishing.
他们喜欢钓鱼。
Sometimes they caught a lot of fish.
有时他们钓到很多鱼。
Sometimes they caught a couple of fish.
有时他们钓到几条鱼。
Sometimes they caught no fish.
有时他们钓不到鱼。
But fishing was fun even if they caught no fish.
虽然钓不到鱼，但是钓鱼本身很有乐趣。
The boat left the dock.
船驶出了码头。
The boat stopped in the middle of the lake.
船在湖中心停下。
Everyone put worms on their hooks.
每个人都在鱼钩上放上鱼饵。
Some people put live worms on their hooks.
有些人在鱼钩上放上活虫子。
Some people put dead worms on their hooks.
有些人在鱼钩上放上死虫子。
Some people put rubber worms on their hooks.
有些人在鱼钩上放上橡胶虫子。
Everyone dropped their hooks into the water.
所有人都把鱼钩甩到水里。
Then they waited.
然后他们等着。
They waited for the fish to bite the worms.
他们等着鱼来咬虫子。
