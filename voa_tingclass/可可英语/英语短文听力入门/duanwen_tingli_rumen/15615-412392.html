His laptop sat on his desk.
他的笔记本电脑在他的办公桌上。
His laptop always overheated.
他的笔记本电脑总是过热。
It always overheated quickly.
它总是很快就热了。
How could he keep it cool?
怎么能保持他凉呢?
He turned on his living room fan.
他打开客厅的风扇。
It cooled his laptop.
它吹着他的笔记本电脑。
But the fan blew dust around.
但风扇吹得到处是灰尘。
It blew paper around.
它吹得到处是纸。
He turned off his living room fan.
他关掉了客厅的风扇。
He thought. He thought some more.
他想，他想了好多。
How could he keep his laptop cool?
怎么能使他的笔记本电脑降温呢?
He had an idea.
他有了一个主意。
He bought little plastic blocks.
他买了小塑料块。
Each block was one inch square.
每一块是一英寸。
He put the laptop on top of the blocks.
他把笔记本放在塑料块上。
One block was under each corner of the laptop.
一个塑料块在笔记本电脑的一个脚上。
Now the laptop sat a little above the desk.
现在笔记本电脑上坐着一个小桌子上。
There was space under the laptop.
笔记本电脑下面有了空间。
There was space for cool air to flow.
空气在空间中流动。
The cool air prevented overheating.
凉爽的空气防止过热。
