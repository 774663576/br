Lulu's mom gave Lulu a pencil.
露露的妈妈给了她一支铅笔。
It was a yellow pencil.
那是一支黄色铅笔。
It was a number two pencil.
那是二号铅笔。
It had a pink eraser.
上面有个粉色橡皮。
The eraser was small and round and pink.
橡皮小小的，圆圆的，粉红色的。
Lulu's mom gave Lulu a piece of paper.
露露的妈妈给了她一张纸。
It was a white piece of paper.
那是张白纸。
There were blue lines on the white paper.
白纸上面有蓝线。
Lulu wrote a letter to her grandma.
露露给姥姥写了一封信。
Her letter said, "Dear Grandma, Hello, I love you."
她在信里写道，“亲爱的姥姥，你好，我爱你。”
Lulu told her mom she was done.
露露告诉妈妈她写完了。
Her mom looked at the letter.
她妈妈看看这封信。
"This is a short letter," her mom said.
“这是一封简短的信，”她妈妈说。
Lulu said, "Yes, Mom, it is a short letter. It is short but sweet."
露露说，“是的，妈妈，是封简短的信。信很短但充满甜蜜。”
