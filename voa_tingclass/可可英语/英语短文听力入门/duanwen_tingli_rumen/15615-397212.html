Don had a pistol.
唐有把手枪。
It was a small pistol.
那是一把小手枪。
He offered it to his brother Tony.
他把它送给了他弟弟托尼。
Tony looked at the pistol.
托尼看着这把手枪。
It was a beautiful pistol.
手枪很漂亮。
It was black.
它是黑色的。
It had a silver trigger.
它有个银色的扳机。
Tony put his finger on the silver trigger.
托尼把手指放到银色扳机上。
He put the pistol in his pocket.
他把手枪放到衣兜里。
He took it out of his pocket.
他把它拿出衣兜。
He walked outside.
他走到外面。
He saw a bird on a wire.
他看见电线上有只鸟。
He aimed the pistol at the bird.
他用手枪对准这只鸟。
He pulled the trigger.
他扣动扳机。
He said, "If the gun was loaded, I would have killed the bird."
他说，“如果手枪上了子弹，我能打死这只鸟。”
Don said, "I'm glad it wasn't loaded. You shouldn't kill birds."
唐说，“我很高兴它没有子弹。你不应该打死鸟。”
Tony thanked Don for the pistol.
托尼谢谢唐给了他把枪。
"I like this pistol. Thank you," Tony said.
“我喜欢这把枪。谢谢你，”托尼说。
He took the pistol home with him.
他把枪带回了家。
