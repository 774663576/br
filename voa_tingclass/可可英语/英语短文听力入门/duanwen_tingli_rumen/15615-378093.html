George is going to make a salad.
乔治要做一份色拉。
He is going to make a simple salad.
他将制作一份简单的色拉。
It will be a simple salad, but it will also be delicious.
这份色拉很简单，但也很美味。
His salad will have only two ingredients.
他的色拉只有两种材料。
One ingredient is fresh cucumber.
一种材料是新鲜黄瓜。
A cucumber is dark green and long.
黄瓜是深绿色的，长长的。
George will peel the skin off the cucumber.
乔治将把黄瓜皮削掉。
He will throw the skin in the trash.
他将把这些皮扔到垃圾桶。
He won't eat the skin.
他不吃这些皮。
He will slice the cucumber into little pieces.
他将把这根黄瓜切成小碎片。
Each piece will be about the size of a nickel.
每片大概和一个5美分硬币那么大。
He will put all the pieces into a big bowl.
他将把所有的碎片放到一个大碗里。
Then he will pour salad dressing on the pieces in the big bowl.
然后他会把色拉酱调料倒在大碗里的碎黄瓜片上。
That's it-a cucumber, and salad dressing.
完成了-一根黄瓜，一些色拉酱调料。
