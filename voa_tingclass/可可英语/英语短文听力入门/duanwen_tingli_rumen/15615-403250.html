The big room is air-conditioned.
这个大屋子开着空调。
But it smells.
但是很难闻。
Dead bodies are in metal drawers.
尸体放在金属抽屉里。
The bodies have no clothes.
这些尸体都没穿衣服。
The bodies are naked.
这些都是裸体尸体。
A white sheet covers each dead body.
每个尸体上盖着一块白单子。
The drawers slide in.
抽屉推进去。
The drawers slide out.
抽屉拉出来。
A doctor works in the big room with the dead bodies.
一个医生在这个有尸体的大屋子里工作。
He works there almost every day.
几乎每天他都在那里工作。
He cuts the bodies open.
他切开尸体。
He cuts them open with a knife.
他用一把刀把他们切开。
He cuts them open with a saw.
他用锯把他们切开。
He looks at the bodies.
他看看这些尸体。
He looks inside the bodies.
他看看尸体里面。
Why did these people die?
为什么这些人死掉了？
Why did this young man die, he wonders.
为什么这个年轻人死了，他在纳闷。
He tries to answer the question.
他试着去找到问题的答案。
Every dead body is a puzzle.
每个尸体都是一个谜。
The doctor tries to solve the puzzle.
这个医生试图去解决这个谜。
Then he tells the police why the people died.
然后他告诉警察这些人为什么死掉了。
