It was summer.
正是夏天。
Swim classes were beginning.
游泳班开始了。
She didn't know how to swim.
她不知道怎么去游泳。
She wanted to learn.
她想去学。
She called up the pool.
她打电话给游泳池。
"Do you teach adults how to swim?" she asked.
“你们教成人怎么去游泳吗？”她问。
The instructor said, Yes, we teach adults how to swim.
老师说，是的，我们教成人怎么游泳。
We teach kids how to swim.
我们教小孩怎么游泳。
We teach everyone how to swim.
我们教每个人怎么游泳。
She asked how much.
她问多少钱。
"How much do you charge?" she asked.
“你们收费多少？”她问。
"We charge only $25 for adults," the instructor said.
“我们每个成人仅收费25美元，”老师说。
She gave him her name.
她给了他她的姓名。
He put her name on the swim class list.
他把她的姓名写在了游泳班名单上。
"You are on the swim class list," he said.
“你现在在游泳班名单上，”他说。
"Your first lesson is Saturday at noon. Bring $25 and a towel."
“你的第一节课在周六中午。带25美元和一条毛巾。”
She asked, "That's all I need to bring?"
她问，“这就是我需要带的全部？”
He said, "I'm sorry. Bring your swimsuit, too."
他说，“我很抱歉。请也带来你的泳衣。”
