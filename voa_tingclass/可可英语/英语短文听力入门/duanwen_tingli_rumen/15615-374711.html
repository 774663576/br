Joseph never opens the front door.
约瑟夫从不开前门。
When someone knocks on the front door, Joseph says, "Who is it?"
当有人敲前门，约瑟夫说，“谁呀？”
But he does not open the door.
但是他不打开门。
His parents told him to keep the door closed.
他父母告诉她要让门关着。
His parents want him to be safe.
他父母想保证他的安全。
He will be safe if he doesn't open the door.
如果他不开门，他就是安全的。
Yesterday someone knocked on the front door.
昨天有人敲前门。
"Who is it?" Joseph asked.
“谁呀？”约瑟夫问。
"It's the mailman," the person said.
“我是邮递员，”这个人回答。
"I cannot open the door," Joseph said.
“我不能打开门，”约瑟夫说。
"Okay, I will come back tomorrow," the mailman said.
“好吧，明天我再来，”邮递员说。
"Goodbye," Joseph said.
“再见，”约瑟夫说。
Joseph is a good boy.
约瑟夫是个好孩子。
He listens to his parents.
他很听父母的话。
He doesn't open the door for any person.
他从不给别人开门。
