He called up the post office.
他打电话给邮局。
"Where is my package?" he asked.
“我的包裹在哪里?”他问道。
"What is the tracking number?" the postal worker asked.
“什么是跟踪号码吗?”邮政工人问。
He told her the tracking number.
他告诉她的跟踪号码。
She said, "Your package is at the main post office.
她说,“你的包裹在主要邮局。
You can pick it up anytime."
你可以随时把它取回去。”
He said, "I don't want to pick it up. Can you deliver it?"
他说,“我不想把它取回来。你能送它吗?”
She said yes. "We will deliver it on Monday,"she said.
她说,是的。“我们将在周一送。”她说。
"Will you be home?"
“你会在家吗?”
He said, "What time on Monday?"
他说,“周一什么时候?”
She said, "Anytime between 8 a.m. and 5 p.m."
她说,“在上午8点到下午5点。”
He said, "I have to work. I can't stay home all day."
他说,“我得工作。我不能整天呆在家里。”
She said, "That's okay. We can deliver it on Tuesday."
她说,“没关系。我们可以周二送。”
He said, "I have to work on Tuesday, too. Please deliver it on Saturday."
他说:“我周二必须工作。请周六送。”
