The bathroom wall had a nail in it.
卫生间墙上有个钉子。
He pulled the nail out of the wall.
他把钉子从墙上拔出来。
It was a big nail.
那是个大钉子。
When he pulled the nail out, a hole was in the wall.
当他把钉子拔出来时，墙上出现了一个洞。
It was a big hole.
那是个大洞。
He wanted to repair the hole.
他想把洞堵上。
He went to Wal-Mart.
他去了沃尔玛。
Wal-Mart has everything for sale.
沃尔玛出售各类商品。
Wal-Mart is a very popular store.
沃尔玛是个很受欢迎的商店。
He asked a clerk, "How can I fix a nail hole in my bathroom wall?"
他问一个职员，“我怎么才能把我卫生间上的钉子洞补上？”
The clerk said it was very simple.
职员说很简单。
A nail hole was easy to fix.
一个钉子洞很容易补上。
"Just buy this tube of Nail Hole Filler. Then squeeze it into the hole," the clerk said.
“买管这种钉孔填料。然后把它挤进洞里，”这个职员说。
He took the filler home.
他把填料带回家。
He squeezed it into the hole.
他把它挤进洞里。
He let the filler dry overnight.
他让填料整晚干透。
The next day, there was no hole!
第二天，洞没有了！
