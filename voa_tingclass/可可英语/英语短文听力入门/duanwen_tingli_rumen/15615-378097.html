Kenneth cleaned his apartment.
肯尼思打扫了他的公寓。
He emptied the trash.
他把垃圾桶清理了。
He washed the dirty dishes.
他洗洗脏盘子。
He looked in his bathroom.
他看看厕所。
The sink and bathtub were dirty.
洗涤槽和浴缸都很脏。
He scrubbed the sink and bathtub.
他把洗涤槽和浴缸都擦洗了。
He looked in his bedroom.
他看看卧室。
Clothes were on the floor.
衣服都在地板上。
He picked up the clothes.
他捡起这些衣服。
He put the shirts on hangers.
他把衬衣用衣架撑起来。
He folded the pants.
他把裤子叠起来。
He put the pants in the dresser.
他把裤子放进衣柜。
He washed the dirty clothes.
他洗洗脏衣服。
He looked in his living room.
他看看客厅。
Papers were on the floor.
地板上有纸。
Books and newspapers and magazines were on the floor.
书，报纸和杂志都在地板上。
He picked them all up.
他把它们都捡起来。
He put them on the book shelves.
他把它们放到书架上。
Then he vacuumed his whole apartment.
然后他用吸尘器清扫了整个公寓。
