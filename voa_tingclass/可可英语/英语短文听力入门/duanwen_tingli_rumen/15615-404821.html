God takes good people to heaven.
上帝选择好人去天堂。
He wants good people to be with Him.
他想让好人和他在一起。
He leaves bad people on Earth.
他把坏人放到地球上。
He doesn't want bad people near Him.
他不想让坏人接近他。
He lets them do bad things on Earth.
他让他们在地球上做坏事。
When they die, He sends them to the devil.
当他们死后，他把他们送给魔鬼。
The devil makes them work every day.
魔鬼让他们每天工作。
They work next to a big fire.
他们在炉火旁工作。
They are always right next to the fire.
他们总是紧挨着炉火。
They are always hot from the fire.
挨着火他们总是很热。
They carry coal.
他们搬煤。
The devil makes them carry heavy buckets of black coal.
魔鬼让他们搬沉沉的煤桶。
They carry the coal to the fire.
他们把煤搬到炉火上。
They pour the coal on the big fire.
他们把煤倒在大火上。
Then they refill the big buckets with more coal.
然后他们用更多的煤来装满大桶。
They never get a drink of water.
他们从来喝不到一滴水。
They never get to rest.
他们从来得不到休息。
