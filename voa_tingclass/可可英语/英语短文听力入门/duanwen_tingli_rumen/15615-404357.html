He looked at the rising moon.
他看着初升的月亮。
It was rising above the houses.
它正在升到房子上面。
It was big and orange.
它很大，桔黄色。
It looked bigger than the rising sun.
看起来它比初升的太阳大。
How could it be so big?
它怎么会这么大？
How could it be so orange?
它怎么会是桔黄色？
Several hours later, he looked at the moon again.
几个小时后，他再次看看月亮。
Now it was high in the sky.
现在它在高高的天空上。
Now it was smaller.
现在它变小了。
It wasn't orange anymore.
它不再是桔黄色。
Now it was white.
现在它是白色。
What happened?
发生了什么？
It changed size.
它的大小变了。
It changed color.
它的颜色变了。
Before, it was big and orange.
之前，它是大大的橘黄色的。
It was like a big pumpkin.
像一个大大的南瓜。
Now, it was smaller and white.
现在，它是变小了，成了白色的。
It was like a light bulb.
它像一个灯泡。
But it was still pretty.
但是它还是很漂亮。
It was still bright.
它还是那么亮。
It was the brightest light in the sky.
它是天空中最亮的光。
