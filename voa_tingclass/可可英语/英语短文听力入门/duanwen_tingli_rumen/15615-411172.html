It was time for the annual blood drive.
这是年度献血运动的时间。
He told his girlfriend, "I'm going to give blood tomorrow."
他对他的女朋友说:“我明天去献血。”
She said, "Don't do that. You could get a disease."
她说,“不要这样做。你会得一种疾病。”
He asked, "I could get a disease? How could I get a disease?"
他问:“我可以得一种疾病?我怎么能得一种疾病?”
She said, You never know.
她说,你永远不会知道的。
What if a nurse trips and stabs you with a dirty needle?
比如护士犯错以及刺穿你用脏的针头?
He said that was ridiculous.
他说这是荒谬的。
Nurses don't trip.
护士不会弄错。
Nurses don't stab blood donors with dirty needles.
护士不会用肮脏的针头刺伤献血者。
He went to the blood drive the next day.
第二天他去了献血运动。
He watched the nurses carefully.
他仔细地看着护士。
None of them tripped.
没有一个人犯错。
None of them stabbed a blood donor with a dirty needle.
没有人用脏的针头刺伤一个献血者。
He gave blood.
他献了血。
He told his girlfriend that no one had tripped and stabbed him.
他告诉他的女朋友,没有人犯错刺伤他。
She said he was lucky this time.
她说他这次是幸运的。
