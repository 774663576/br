Pat looked at the washer.
帕特看看洗衣机。
The washer had dials on it.
洗衣机有按钮。
It had two dials on it.
上面有两个按钮。
There were words next to each dial.
每个按钮旁都有文字。
Each dial had three words next to it.
每个按钮旁都有3个字。
The words next to one dial were Small, Medium, and Large.
每个按钮旁的字都是小，中，大。
This dial was for the size of the load.
这个按钮表示负荷量。
Did Pat have a small load, a medium load, or a large load?
帕特有少量，中量或者大量衣服要洗？
The words next to the other dial were Cold, Warm, and Hot.
另一个按钮旁的字是冷，温，热。
Did Pat want to wash her clothes in cold water, warm water, or hot water?
帕特用冷水，温水还是热水洗了她的衣服？
Pat turned one dial to Large, and the other dial to Hot.
帕特把一个按钮调到大，另一调到热。
Then she pushed the Start button on the washer.
然后她按了洗衣机开始按钮。
