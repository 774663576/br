Davy was 10.
戴维10岁了。
His dad was driving the car.
他爸爸在开车。
The radio was on.
收音机开着。
His dad was listening to the radio.
他爸爸正在听收音机。
The man on the radio was talking about an accident.
收音机上的男的正在谈论一起事故。
The accident was on the freeway.
事故发生在告诉公路上。
Two people were dead.
死了两个人。
The accident had just happened 30 minutes ago.
事故发生在30分钟前。
His dad told him that life can be short.
他爸爸告诉他生命很短暂。
Accidents happen everywhere.
到处都会发生事故。
They happen to everyone.
事故会发生在任何人身上。
"You must always be careful, Davy," his dad said.
“你必须时刻小心，戴维，”他爸爸说。
"Never be in a hurry. Always pay attention to other drivers."
“永远不要着急。要一直注意其他司机。”
Davy said he would be a careful driver.
戴维说他会成为一个很小心的司机。
He would never be in a hurry.
他永远都不会着急。
He would always pay attention.
他也会一直很注意。
"Can I drive the car now?" he asked his dad.
“我现在能开车吗？”他问爸爸。
