She offered her car to her brother.
她给哥哥提供了辆车。
He said he would think about it.
他说他会考虑一下。
"Let me think about it," he said.
他说：“让我想想。”
She called him up a week later.
一周后，她给他打了电话。
"Do you want my car for free?" she asked.
她问道：“你想免费拥有我的座驾吗？”
He said, "I'm thinking about it."
他说：“我还在考虑。”
She asked, "How long do you have to think about it?"
她问：“你需要考虑多久？”
He said he didn't know.
他说他不知道。
"Let me think about it some more," he said.
他说：“让我再想一想。”
She called up a charity.
她给慈善机构打了电话。
She said, "Do you want my car for free?"
她说：“你想无偿拥有我的汽车吗？”
The man at the charity said yes.
该男子说想。
She asked, "You don't have to think about it?"
她问：“你不需要再考虑一下了？”
He said, "No, I don't have to think about it. Who has to think about a free car?"
他说：“不用，谁会为一辆免费车考虑。”
He picked up her car the next day.
第二天，他把车提走了。
