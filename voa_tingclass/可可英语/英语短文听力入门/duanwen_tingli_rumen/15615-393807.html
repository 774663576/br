He had very little money.
他没多少钱。
It was cold outside.
外面很冷。
It was winter.
那是冬天。
But he was inside.
但是他在里面。
He was inside his apartment.
他在他的公寓里面。
Right now he was okay, because he was inside his apartment.
现在他很好，因为他在他的公寓里面。
But next month he would not be okay.
但是下个月他就不会这么好了。
Next month he would be outside his apartment.
下个月他将离开他的公寓。
He had lost his job.
他失业了。
He couldn't pay his rent.
他付不起房租。
He had called his landlord.
他给房东打了电话。
He asked his landlord if he could stay an extra month for free.
他问房东自己是否可以免费多住一个月。
His landlord said no.
他的房东拒绝了。
His landlord said, "If you can't pay the rent, you must go."
他的房东说，“你如果付不起房租，你就离开。”
Where could he go?
他能去哪里呢？
He would sleep in his car.
他可以睡在车里。
But a car is a cold place in the winter.
但是在冬天睡车里很冷。
He was very sad.
他很难过。
