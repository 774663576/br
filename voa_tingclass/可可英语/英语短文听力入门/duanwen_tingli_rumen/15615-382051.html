Danny looked at his brown shoes.
丹尼看着自己的棕色鞋子。
They looked old and dirty.
它们看起来又旧又脏。
They needed a shine.
该擦擦这双鞋了。
It was time to shine his brown shoes.
是时候该擦擦他的棕色鞋子了。
He got a rag.
他拿起一块抹布。
He got a shoe brush.
他准备了一个鞋刷。
He got a can of polish.
他准备了一管鞋油。
He opened the can of polish.
他打开鞋油盖。
He put the rag around his finger.
他把抹布缠到手上。
He scooped out a bit of polish with his finger.
他用手挖出一点鞋油。
He rubbed the polish all over the left shoe.
他把左鞋用鞋油擦了擦。
He brushed the left shoe.
他刷刷左鞋。
He brushed it and brushed it.
他刷啊刷啊。
The shoe began to shine.
鞋变亮了。
It looked like a new shoe.
看起来像只新鞋子。
Then Danny put polish on the right shoe.
然后丹尼给右鞋打鞋油。
He brushed the right shoe until it looked like a new shoe.
他刷刷右鞋，一直到它看起来像只新鞋子。
