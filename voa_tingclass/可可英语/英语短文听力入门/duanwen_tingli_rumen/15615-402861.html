Betty was excited.
贝蒂很兴奋。
It was December 1.
这是12月1号。
Christmas Day was only 24 days away.
圣诞节还有24天就到了。
She was looking at a magazine.
她在看一本杂志。
It was a magazine for little girls.
那是一本专为小女孩儿出版的杂志。
There were many pictures in the magazine.
杂志上有好多图片。
There were many pictures of dolls.
有很多洋娃娃的图片。
The dolls were from many countries.
洋娃娃来自很多国家。
There were dolls from Mexico.
有来自墨西哥的洋娃娃。
There were dolls from China.
有来自中国的洋娃娃。
There were dolls from France.
有来自法国的洋娃娃。
There were dolls from everywhere.
有来自各个地方的洋娃娃。
There were so many dolls.
有太多的洋娃娃。
She showed the pictures to her mom.
她给妈妈看看这些图片。
"Mommy, will Santa bring me all these dolls?" she asked.
“妈妈，圣诞老人会把所有这些洋娃娃都带给我吗？”她问。
"No, honey," her mom said, "Santa will bring you only one doll. So pick the one you like best."
“不会的，甜心，”她妈妈说，“圣诞老人只能给你带一个洋娃娃。所以挑一个你最喜欢的。”
"But, Mommy," she said, "I like them ALL the best."
“但是，妈妈，”她说，“所有的我都喜欢”
