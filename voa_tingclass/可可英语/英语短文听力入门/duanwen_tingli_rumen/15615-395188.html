He was bored.
他很烦。
He wanted to do something fun.
他想做些有趣的事情。
He wanted to do something fun with his friend.
他想和他的朋友做些有趣的事情。
He called up his friend.
他给他朋友打电话。
"I'm bored. Let's do something fun!" he said.
“我很烦。让我们做些有趣的事吧！”他说。
His friend said, "That sounds great. I'm glad you called. I'm bored, too. What do you want to do?"
他朋友说，“听起来很棒。很高兴你打电话过来。我也很烦。你想做什么？”
He said he wanted to go to the beach.
他说他想去海边。
His friend said that sounded great.
他朋友说听起来不错。
He picked up his friend in his car.
他开车接上他朋友。
They went to the beach.
他们去了海边。
They threw a Frisbee to each other.
他们扔飞盘玩。
They swam in the ocean.
他们在大海里游泳。
They built a big sand castle.
他们建造了一座很大的沙子城堡。
They watched the sailboats.
他们着帆船。
They watched the sea gulls.
他们看海鸥。
He drove his friend home.
他开车把朋友送回家。
"That was fun! Let's do it again sometime," his friend said.
“很有趣！抽个时间咱们再做一次，”他朋友说。
