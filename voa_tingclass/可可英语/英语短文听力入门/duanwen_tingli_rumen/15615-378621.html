Steven was hungry.
史蒂芬很饿。
What could he eat?
他要吃点什么呢？
He looked in the refrigerator.
他看看冰箱。
He saw some orange cheese.
他看见一些香橙奶酪
He saw some yellow butter.
他看见一些黄油。
He knew what he would eat.
他直到自己要吃什么了。
He would eat a melted cheese sandwich.
他可以吃一个夹奶酪的三明治。
He took the cheese and butter out of the refrigerator.
他把奶酪和黄油从冰箱里拿出来。
He buttered two slices of bread.
他在两片面包上抹上黄油。
He sliced the cheese and put it between the two buttered slices.
他把奶酪切片，夹在两片抹了黄油的面包中间。
He heated the frying pan.
他加热煎锅。
He put the cheese sandwich into the frying pan.
他把奶酪三明治放入煎锅。
After three minutes, he flipped the sandwich over.
三分钟后，他翻翻三明治。
After three more minutes, he put the melted cheese sandwich on a plate.
又过了三分钟，他把奶酪三明治放到了盘子里。
