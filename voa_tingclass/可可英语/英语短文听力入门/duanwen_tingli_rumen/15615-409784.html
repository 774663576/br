"You have to chop down those two trees," said the fire control agent.
“你必须砍下那两棵树，”消防特工说。
"Why do I have to chop down those two trees?" asked Diane.
“我为什么要砍下那两棵树呢？”戴安娜问。
"Because those two trees are a fire hazard," said the fire control agent.
“因为那两棵树是火灾危害，”消防特工说。
"All trees are a fire hazard," said Diane.
“所有的树都是火灾危害，”戴安娜说。
"Yes, but those two trees are a super fire hazard," said the agent.
“是的，但是那两棵树是一个超级火灾危害，”消防特工说。
"Why are those two trees a super fire hazard?" Diane asked.
“为什么那两棵树是超级火灾危害呢？”戴安娜问。
"Because those two trees are right next to your house. Chop them down," he said.
“因为那两棵树正好靠近你的房子。砍下他们，”他说。
Diane was poor.
戴安娜很穷。
She couldn't afford to pay someone to chop the trees down.
她付不起钱让别人把树砍下来。
The agent said, "Chop them down, or I will fine you."
特工说，“砍下他们，或者我将处罚你。”
The next day, she poured gasoline on both trees.
第二天，她把汽油浇在所有的树上。
Then she set them on fire.
然后她把他们都点上火了。
