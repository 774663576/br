Dotty looked at the door.
多蒂看看门。
It was a closet door.
那是个壁橱门。
The door had a silver handle.
门有银色把手。
The silver handle was round.
银色把手是圆形的。
The round, silver handle was a doorknob.
这个圆形的，银色手柄是个门把手。
One day she tried to turn the doorknob.
一天她试图打开门把手。
She tried to open the closet door.
她试图打开壁橱门。
But the doorknob didn't turn.
但是壁橱门拧不开。
She couldn't open the closet door.
她打不开壁橱门。
The closet door wouldn't open.
壁橱门打不开。
The door was locked.
壁橱门是锁着的。
"Why is the door locked?" Dotty asked her mom.
“为什么壁橱门是锁着的？”多蒂问妈妈。
Her mom said it was locked for her safety.
她妈妈说锁着壁橱门是为了她的安全。
"What's behind the door?" she asked her mom.
“门后面是什么？”她问妈妈。
Her mom said a rifle was behind the locked door.
她妈妈说门后有把步枪。
Daddy kept a rifle in the closet.
爸爸在壁橱里藏了一把步枪。
