Dad took his son Chris to the baseball game.
爸爸把他儿子克里斯带去看棒球比赛。
The Los Angeles Dodgers were playing the San Francisco Giants.
洛杉矶道奇队正和旧金山巨人队打比赛。
The Dodgers were the home team.
道奇队是主场球队。
The Giants were the visiting team.
巨人队是客场球队。
Dad and Chris walked into Dodger Stadium.
爸爸和克里斯走进到齐体育场。
Many people were there.
那里有很多人。
Most of them wanted to see the Dodgers win.
大部分人想要道奇队赢。
They wanted to see the Giants lose.
他们想要巨人队输。
Dad and Chris found their seats.
爸爸和克里斯找到座位。
They sat down.
他们坐下来。
The game had already started.
比赛已经开始了。
Chris told his dad he was hungry.
克里斯告诉爸爸自己很饿。
His dad bought two bags of peanuts for Chris.
他爸爸给克里斯买了两袋花生。
He bought two hot dogs for Chris.
他给克里斯买了两个热狗。
He bought a big soda for Chris.
他给克里斯买了一大瓶汽水。
A foul ball came their way.
一个界外球朝他们飞过来。
People dived for the foul ball.
人们扑向这个界外球。
They knocked Chris' soda over.
他们把克里斯的汽水打翻了。
His dad bought him another soda.
他爸爸给他又买了一瓶汽水。
