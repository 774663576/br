A new neighbor moved in.
一个新邻居搬进来了。
Will the new neighbor be quiet, Liz wondered.
新邻居会不会安静呢，丽兹想。
The new neighbor wasn't quiet.
新邻居不安静。
The new neighbor was loud.
新邻居很吵。
He played his TV loud.
他看电视很大声音。
He played his music loud.
他听音乐很大声音。
He talked on the phone loud.
他通电话很大声音。
Loud, loud, loud!
很吵，很吵，很吵！
He slammed the door when he left his apartment.
当他离开公寓的时候，砰的一声关上了门。
He slammed the door when he entered his apartment.
当他走进他的公寓时，砰的一声关上了门。
Slam, slam, slam! He had a basketball.
大满贯，满贯，满贯！他有一个篮球。
He bounced the basketball everywhere.
他到处打篮球。
He bounced it on his apartment floor.
他把它弹在他的公寓地板上。
He bounced it against his apartment wall.
他把它弹起来，弹到他的公寓墙上。
He bounced it off his apartment ceiling.
他把它弹到公寓的天花板上掉了下来。
Bounce, bounce, bounce!
弹跳，弹跳，弹跳！
Liz asked him to be quiet.
丽兹让他安静点。
"Please be quiet," she asked.
“请安静点，”她说。
"I will not be quiet," he said.
“我不会安静的，”他说。
Liz wanted to kill the new neighbor.
丽兹想去杀了这个新邻居。
Kill, kill, kill!
杀，杀，杀！
