There was something wrong with his leg.
他的腿出了毛病。
It burned.
它被烧伤了。
But it burned in one spot only.
但是它只被烧了一块。
He didn't know what was wrong.
他不知道怎么回事。
The spot was smaller than a dime.
烧伤面小于一个硬币。
It was on the back of his leg.
烧伤的地方在他的腿背侧。
He picked up a hand mirror.
他拿起一个手镜。
He used the hand mirror to look at the back of his leg.
他用手镜看看腿背侧。
He saw a small lump.
他看到一个小肿块。
He put his finger on the lump.
他把手指放到肿块上。
The lump was hard.
肿块很硬。
It wasn't a soft lump.
那不是个软肿块。
The lump felt like a small stone.
肿块摸起来像是个小石头。
It was like a small stone under his skin.
像是个小石头在他的皮肤下面。
But it was like a small stone that was on fire.
但是它像是个正在燃烧的小石头。
It burned.
它被烧到了。
Something was wrong.
有些不对劲儿。
He needed to see a doctor.
他需要看医生。
