If you are looking for houseplants that do not take up a lot of space and are easy to grow, air plants might be the right choice.
如果你正在寻找既不占用很大空间，又能轻松种活的室内植物，那空气植物可能是绝佳选择。
There are more than 500 different species of air plants. Officially they are known as Tillandsia.
有500多种不同的空气植物。它们的官方名称叫“空气凤梨”。
These plants are native to places in North and South America that support year-round plant growth.
这些植物原产于北美和南美洲的一些地方，这些地方全年都有植物可以生长。
Air plants belong to the same family as the pineapple.
空气植物与菠萝同属一科。
Instead of soil, these plants attach themselves to things like trees, rocks and buildings.
这些植物不需要土壤，而是附着在树木、岩石和建筑物等东西上。
They do not cause problems for the objects they grow on. Air plants do not have roots.
它们不会给寄生的物体带来不良影响。空气植物没有根。
They depend on specialized cells – called trichomes – to permit their leaves to take in water and nutrients.
它们依靠特殊的细胞--称为毛状体--来让叶子吸收水分和营养。
Associated Press gardening writer Jessica Damiano has experimented with air plants and offers these guidelines for people wanting to grow them.
美联社园艺作家杰西卡·达米亚诺对空气植物进行了实验，并为想要种植它们的人提供了以下指南。
Air plants require bright, indirect light.
空气植物需要明亮的间接光线。
So, they should be placed near an east- or west-facing window, but not directly in front of it.
因此，应该把它们放置在朝东或朝西的窗户附近，但不能直接放在窗户跟前。
Direct sunlight can burn the plant's leaves.
阳光直射会灼伤植物的叶子。
In homes with average humidity levels, put air plants in a bowl of room-temperature water once a week for 30 to 60 minutes.
在湿度水平一般的家庭中，每周要有一次把空气植物泡在室温水中30至60分钟。
In homes with drier air, this process can be repeated when needed.
在空气比较干燥的家庭，可以根据需要，多泡水几次。
Lightly shake the plants to remove extra water.
轻轻摇动植物以除去多余的水分。
Then, place them upside-down on a cloth to dry for several hours.
然后，把它们倒放在布上晾干几个小时。
This step is necessary to prevent water from collecting on the plant's leaves, which can cause damage.
这个步骤很必要，可以防止水分积聚在植物的叶子上，给它们造成损害。
If using chlorinated water, it is best to let the liquid sit for 12 to 24 hours to permit the chemical to dissipate before watering plants.
如果你使用的是含氯水，最好先让水静置12到24小时，让里面的氯在给植物浇水前消散掉。
Air plants are especially sensitive to chlorine.
空气植物对氯特别敏感。
Growers should feed their air plants with any all-purpose houseplant, bromeliad or orchid fertilizer.
种植者应该用任何室内植物、凤梨科植物或兰花肥料来喂养空气植物。
The substance should be added to the soaking water monthly to provide added nutrients.
应该每月往浸泡水中添加这些物质，给它们提供额外的营养。
Use about half the amount suggested by the fertilizer manufacturer.
用量约为化肥制造商建议用量的一半。
Air plants bloom only once.
空气植物只开一次花。
The resulting pink, coral, purple or white flowers are a signal that the plants are nearing death.
那些粉色、珊瑚色、紫色或白色的花是植物濒临死亡的信号。
However, air plants produce offshoots, known as "pups."
不过，空气植物会产生分支，相当于我们熟知的“幼崽”。
These can be separated into new plants once they grow to about one-third the size of the parent.
等它们长到亲本约三分之一大小的时候，就可以分离成新的植物。
Notable air plants include Spanish moss which can be found hanging from large trees in tropical or subtropical areas.
很出名的空气植物有西班牙苔藓，能悬挂生长在热带或亚热带地区的大树上。
In the home, Spanish moss is grown in a humid environment, such as a bathroom.
在家里，西班牙苔藓会生长在潮湿的环境中，比如卫生间里。
Another kind is Tillandsia ionantha, which is also known as the sky plant.
另一种是小精灵，也被称为空中植物。
It is a shaggy-looking plant with green bracts.
这种植物很蓬松，有绿色的苞片。
Bracts are leaf-like structures that grow from the area just beneath a flower.
苞片是一种叶状结构，从花的正下方生长出来。
The tops of bracts can turn pink or purple before flowering.
在开花前，苞片的顶部可能会变成粉红色或紫色。
Tillandsia caput-medusae is named for its similarity to the snake-haired head of Medusa, from Greek mythology.
小精灵美杜莎头因与希腊神话中长着一头蛇发的美杜莎相似而得名。
The plant is popular because of its long, wavy leaves that grow upward from a fat, round base.
这种植物很受欢迎，它那长长的波状叶子会从肥大的圆形根基向上生长。
Perhaps the most unusual species of Tillandsia is the Xerographic air plant.
或许小精灵中最不寻常的物种是Xerographic空中植物。
This kind is known as the King of Air Plants. It can grow up to about one-half meter.
这种植物被称为空气植物之王。它能长到约半米长。
The plant's long, thin silver leaves curl downward to produce a round shape that sets it apart from other air plants.
这种植物又长又细的银色叶子会向下卷曲，形成圆形，跟其他的空气植物不一样。
You can place air plants in just about any spot that provides the required sunlight and good air flow.
只要有阳光和良好的空气流通，你几乎可以把空气植物放在任何位置。
Glass containers or terrariums are popular choices.
玻璃容器或水晶盒是很受欢迎的选择。
But growers can also put air plants inside seashells or on rocks.
不过人们也可以把空气植物种在贝壳里或岩石上。
One could even create a floating Tillandsia garden by hanging several plants from fishing line.
人们甚至可以把空气植物悬挂在钓鱼线上，创造出漂浮的小精灵花园。
The plants can also be attached to natural wood pieces with wire or glue.
还可以用金属丝或胶水把这些植物固定在天然木片上。
Some choose to grow them inside hanging frames.
有人会把它们种在悬挂的框架里。
When choosing placement possibilities, the main thing to keep in mind is to make sure you are fully meeting all the plant's needs.
在选择安置方案时，主要要牢记，确保你满足了植物的所有需求。
